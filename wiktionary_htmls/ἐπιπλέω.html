Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

ἐπῐ- (epi-, “on, over”) +‎ πλέω (pléō, “to sail”)

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /e.pi.plé.ɔː/ → /e.piˈple.o/ → /e.piˈple.o/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /e.pi.plé.ɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /e.piˈple.o/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /e.piˈple.o/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /e.piˈple.o/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /e.piˈple.o/

Verb

[edit]

ἐπῐπλέω • (epipléō)

  1. to sail on or over (a body of water)
  2. to sail on board (a ship)
  3. to sail against, attack
