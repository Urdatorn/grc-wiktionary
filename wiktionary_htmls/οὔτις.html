Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

From οὐ (ou, “not”) +‎ τῐς (tis, “somebody”).

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /ǔː.tis/ → /ˈu.tis/ → /ˈu.tis/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /ǔː.tis/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /ˈu.tis/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /ˈu.tis/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /ˈu.tis/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /ˈu.tis/
