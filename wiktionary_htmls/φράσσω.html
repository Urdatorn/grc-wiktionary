Ancient Greek

[edit]

Alternative forms

[edit]
  • φρᾰ́ττω (phráttō) — Attic

Etymology

[edit]

Unclear. The traditional connection with Latin farciō is quite uncertain.

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /pʰrás.sɔː/ → /ˈɸras.so/ → /ˈfra.so/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /pʰrás.sɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /ˈpʰras.so/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /ˈɸras.so/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /ˈfras.so/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /ˈfra.so/

Verb

[edit]

φρᾰ́σσω • (phrássō)

  1. to fence in, hedge around, secure, fortify
    • 800 BCE – 600 BCE, Homer, Iliad 12.263
    • 800 BCE – 600 BCE, Homer, Odyssey 5.256
    • 458 BCE, Aeschylus, Agamemnon 1375
    • 472 BCE, Aeschylus, The Persians 456
    • 522 BCE – 443 BCE, Pindar, Isthmian Ode 1.95
    • 800 BCE – 600 BCE, Homer, Iliad 15.566, (Compare Aeschylus, Seven Against Thebes 63)
    • 460 BCE – 420 BCE, Herodotus, Histories 8.51
    • 467 BCE, Aeschylus, Seven Against Thebes 798
    • 460 BCE – 420 BCE, Herodotus, Histories 9.70
    • 460 BCE – 395 BCE, Thucydides, History of the Peloponnesian War 8.35
    • 800 BCE – 600 BCE, Homer, Iliad 17.268, (Compare Herodotus, Histories 7.142, Euripides, Iphigenia in Aulis 826)
    • 460 BCE – 420 BCE, Herodotus, Histories 5.34
    • 460 BCE – 395 BCE, Thucydides, History of the Peloponnesian War 1.82
    • 497 BCE – 405 BCE, Sophocles, Fragments 376
    • 442 BCE, Sophocles, Antigone 235
  2. to put up as a fence
    • 800 BCE – 600 BCE, Homer, Iliad 13.130
    • 460 BCE – 420 BCE, Herodotus, Histories 9.61
    • 458 BCE, Aeschylus, Agamemnon 823
    1. (of dogs) put down one's tail
      • 430 BCE – 354 BCE, Xenophon, Cynegeticus 3.5
  3. to stop up, block
    • 460 BCE – 420 BCE, Herodotus, Histories 8.7
    • 460 BCE – 395 BCE, Thucydides, History of the Peloponnesian War 4.13
    • 384 BCE – 322 BCE, Demosthenes 520.19
    • 460 BCE – 420 BCE, Herodotus, Histories 2.99
    • 360 BCE, Plato, Timaeus 84D
    1. (figuratively) to bar, stop
      • 250 CE – 350 CE, Athenaeus, Deipnosophistae 157D
      • New Testament, Epistle to the Romans 3:19, (Compare Second Epistle to the Corinthians 11:10)
