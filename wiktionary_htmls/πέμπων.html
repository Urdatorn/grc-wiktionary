Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /pém.pɔːn/ → /ˈpem.pon/ → /ˈpem.bon/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /pém.pɔːn/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /ˈpem.pon/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /ˈpem.pon/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /ˈpem.pon/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /ˈpem.bon/

Participle

[edit]

πέμπων • (pémpōn) m (feminine πέμπουσᾰ, neuter πέμπον); first/third declension

  1. present active participle of πέμπω (pémpō)
