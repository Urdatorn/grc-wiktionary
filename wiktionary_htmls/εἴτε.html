Ancient Greek

[edit]

Alternative forms

[edit]
  • αἴτε (aíte) — Doric
  • εἴθ’ (eíth’) — apocopic before /h/

Etymology

[edit]

From εἰ (ei, “if”) +‎ τε (te, “even, and”).

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /ěː.te/ → /ˈi.te/ → /ˈi.te/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /ěː.te/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /ˈi.te/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /ˈi.te/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /ˈi.te/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /ˈi.te/
