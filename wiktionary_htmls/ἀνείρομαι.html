Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

ἀνα- (ana-) +‎ εἴρομαι (eíromai), Ionic variant of ἔρομαι (éromai, “to ask”).

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /a.něː.ro.mai̯/ → /aˈni.ro.mɛ/ → /aˈni.ro.me/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /a.něː.ro.mai̯/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /aˈni.ro.mɛ/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /aˈni.ro.mɛ/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /aˈni.ro.me/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /aˈni.ro.me/

Verb

[edit]

ἀνείρομαι • (aneíromai)

  1. to inquire, ask
    Synonym: ἀνερωτάω (anerōtáō)
