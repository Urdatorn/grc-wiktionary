Ancient Greek

[edit]

Alternative forms

[edit]
  • ὅσσος (hóssos) — Epic

Etymology

[edit]

From Proto-Hellenic *hótsos, from earlier *yótyos, from Proto-Indo-European *yoti, adverb from *yós, whence ὅς (hós).[1]

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /hó.sos/ → /ˈo.sos/ → /ˈo.sos/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /hó.sos/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /ˈ(h)o.sos/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /ˈo.sos/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /ˈo.sos/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /ˈo.sos/
