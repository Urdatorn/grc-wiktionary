Ancient Greek

[edit]

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /ksy.neː.dɔ̌ːs/ → /ksy.niˈðos/ → /ksi.niˈðos/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /ksy.neː.dɔ̌ːs/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /ksy.niˈdos/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /ksy.niˈðos/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /ksy.niˈðos/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /ksi.niˈðos/

Participle

[edit]

ξῠνειδώς • (xuneidṓs) m (feminine ξῠνειδυῖᾰ, neuter ξῠνειδός); first/third declension

  1. perfect active participle of ξύνοιδα (xúnoida)

Noun

[edit]

ξῠνειδώς • (xuneidṓs) m (genitive ξῠνειδότος); third declension

  1. (Old Attic) accomplice
