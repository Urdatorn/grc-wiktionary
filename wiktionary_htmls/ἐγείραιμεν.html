Ancient Greek

[edit]

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /e.ɡěː.rai̯.men/ → /eˈʝi.rɛ.men/ → /eˈʝi.re.men/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /e.ɡěː.rai̯.men/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /eˈɡi.rɛ.men/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /eˈʝi.rɛ.men/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /eˈʝi.re.men/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /eˈʝi.re.men/

Verb

[edit]

ἐγείραιμεν • (egeíraimen)

  1. first-person plural aorist active optative of ἐγείρω (egeírō)
