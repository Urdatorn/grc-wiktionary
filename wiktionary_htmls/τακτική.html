Ancient Greek

[edit]

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /tak.ti.kɛ̌ː/ → /tak.tiˈci/ → /tak.tiˈci/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /tak.ti.kɛ̌ː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /tak.tiˈke̝/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /tak.tiˈci/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /tak.tiˈci/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /tak.tiˈci/

Adjective

[edit]

τακτῐκή • (taktikḗ)

  1. feminine nominative/vocative singular of τακτικός (taktikós)

Noun

[edit]

τακτῐκή • (taktikḗ) f (genitive τακτῐκῆς); first declension

  1. (military) tactics
