Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

From ἐπίκουρος (epíkouros) +‎ -έω (-éō).

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /e.pi.kuː.ré.ɔː/ → /e.pi.kuˈre.o/ → /e.pi.kuˈre.o/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /e.pi.kuː.ré.ɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /e.pi.kuˈre.o/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /e.pi.kuˈre.o/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /e.pi.kuˈre.o/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /e.pi.kuˈre.o/

Verb

[edit]

ἐπῐκουρέω • (epikouréō)

  1. to act as an ally
  2. to aid, help
