Noun

[edit]

φλόγᾰ • (phlóga)

  1. accusative singular of φλόξ (phlóx)

Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

From the Ancient Greek φλόξ (phlóx).

Pronunciation

[edit]
  • IPA(key): [ˈflɔɣa]
  • Hyphenation: φλό‧γα

Noun

[edit]

φλόγα • (flóga) f (plural φλόγες)

  1. flame, blaze
  2. (figuratively) ardour
  3. (card games) straight flush
