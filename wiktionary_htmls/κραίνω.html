  • IPA(key): /krǎi̯.nɔː/ → /ˈkrɛ.no/ → /ˈkre.no/

Verb

[edit]

κραίνω • (kraínō)

  1. to accomplish, perform, fulfill
    • 800 BCE – 600 BCE, Homer, Iliad 1.504:
      τόδε μοι κρήηνον ἐέλδωρ
      tóde moi krḗēnon eéldōr
      grant me this wish
