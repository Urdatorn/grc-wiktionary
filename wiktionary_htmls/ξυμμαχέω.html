Ancient Greek

[edit]

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /ksym.ma.kʰé.ɔː/ → /ksym.maˈçe.o/ → /ksi.maˈçe.o/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /ksym.ma.kʰé.ɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /ksym.maˈkʰe.o/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /ksym.maˈçe.o/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /ksym.maˈçe.o/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /ksi.maˈçe.o/

Verb

[edit]

ξυμμᾰχέω • (xummakhéō)

  1. Old Attic form of συμμαχέω (summakhéō, “to be an ally, to be in alliance”)
