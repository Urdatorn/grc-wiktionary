Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

προ- (pro-) +‎ ὁράω (horáō)

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /pro.o.rá.ɔː/ → /pro.oˈra.o/ → /pro.oˈra.o/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /pro.o.rá.ɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /pro.oˈra.o/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /pro.oˈra.o/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /pro.oˈra.o/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /pro.oˈra.o/

Verb

[edit]

προοράω • (prooráō)

  1. to see before one, see what is just before the eyes
  2. to foresee
  3. to provide, make provision for
