Ancient Greek

[edit]

Alternative forms

[edit]
  • ϝεργάδδομαι (wergáddomai) — Laconian, Cretan

Etymology

[edit]

From ἔργον (érgon, “work”) +‎ -άζω (-ázō).

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /er.ɡáz.do.mai̯/ → /erˈɣa.zo.mɛ/ → /erˈɣa.zo.me/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /er.ɡáz.do.mai̯/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /erˈɡa.zo.mɛ/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /erˈɣa.zo.mɛ/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /erˈɣa.zo.me/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /erˈɣa.zo.me/
