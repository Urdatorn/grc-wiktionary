Ancient Greek

[edit]

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /ɡi.nɔ̌ːs.kɔː/ → /ʝiˈnos.ko/ → /ʝiˈnos.ko/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /ɡi.nɔ̌ːs.kɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /ɡiˈnos.ko/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /ʝiˈnos.ko/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /ʝiˈnos.ko/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /ʝiˈnos.ko/

Verb

[edit]

γῐνώσκω • (ginṓskō)

  1. Ionic and Koine form of γιγνώσκω (gignṓskō)
