Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

ἀνα- (ana-) +‎ οἰκίζω (oikízō)

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /a.noi̯.kíz.dɔː/ → /a.nyˈci.zo/ → /a.niˈci.zo/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /a.noi̯.kíz.dɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /a.nyˈki.zo/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /a.nyˈci.zo/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /a.nyˈci.zo/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /a.niˈci.zo/

Verb

[edit]

ἀνοικίζω • (anoikízō)

  1. to remove (especially to a higher location)
  2. to move (one’s residence) inland or to higher ground
  3. to migrate
