Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

From ἀπο- (apo-, “from, off”) +‎ κρύπτω (krúptō, “hide”)

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /a.po.krýp.tɔː/ → /a.poˈkryp.to/ → /a.poˈkrip.to/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /a.po.krýp.tɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /a.poˈkryp.to/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /a.poˈkryp.to/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /a.poˈkryp.to/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /a.poˈkrip.to/

Verb

[edit]

ἀποκρύπτω • (apokrúptō)

  1. to hide, keep hidden from
  2. to hide from sight, keep hidden, conceal
  3. to obscure
