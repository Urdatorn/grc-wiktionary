Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

From σκηνή (skēnḗ, “tent”) and -θήκη (-thḗkē, “case, box”).

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /skɛː.no.tʰɛ̌ː.kɛː/ → /sci.noˈθi.ci/ → /sci.noˈθi.ci/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /skɛː.no.tʰɛ̌ː.kɛː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /ske̝.noˈtʰe̝.ke̝/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /sci.noˈθi.ci/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /sci.noˈθi.ci/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /sci.noˈθi.ci/

Noun

[edit]

σκηνοθήκη • (skēnothḗkē) f (genitive σκηνοθήκης); first declension

  1. A tent store
