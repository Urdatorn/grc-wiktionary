Ancient Greek

[edit]

Alternative forms

[edit]
  • ὅκου (hókou) — Ionic

Etymology

[edit]

From Proto-Indo-European *kʷos +‎ -ου (-ou).

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /hó.puː/ → /ˈo.pu/ → /ˈo.pu/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /hó.puː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /ˈ(h)o.pu/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /ˈo.pu/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /ˈo.pu/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /ˈo.pu/

Adverb

[edit]

  1. where
