Ancient Greek

[edit]

Alternative forms

[edit]
  • ἔσθω (ésthō) — Poetic
  • ἔδω (édō) — Epic

Etymology

[edit]

Some suggest that this form comes from the original imperative *ἔσθι (*ésthi) of ἔδω (édō), from Proto-Indo-European *h₁ed- (“to eat”).[1]

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /es.tʰí.ɔː/ → /esˈθi.o/ → /esˈθi.o/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /es.tʰí.ɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /esˈtʰi.o/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /esˈθi.o/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /esˈθi.o/
