Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

From κᾰτᾰ- (kata-, “downwards, down; back”) +‎ εἶμῐ (eîmi, “to go, come”).

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /ká.teː.mi/ → /ˈka.ti.mi/ → /ˈka.ti.mi/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /ká.teː.mi/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /ˈka.ti.mi/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /ˈka.ti.mi/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /ˈka.ti.mi/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /ˈka.ti.mi/

Verb

[edit]

κᾰ́τειμῐ • (káteimi)

  1. to go down, come down
    1. to come back, return
    2. (of revenue) to come in
