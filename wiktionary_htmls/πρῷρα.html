Ancient Greek

[edit]

Alternative forms

[edit]
  • πρῴρη (prṓirē) — Epic, Ionic

Etymology

[edit]

From Proto-Hellenic *prṓwařřa, from Proto-Indo-European *préh₃wr̥yh₂.

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /prɔ̂ːi̯.ra/ → /ˈpro.ra/ → /ˈpro.ra/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /prɔ̂ːi̯.ra/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /ˈpro.ra/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /ˈpro.ra/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /ˈpro.ra/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /ˈpro.ra/

Noun

[edit]

πρῷρᾰ • (prôira) f (genitive πρῴρᾱς); first declension

  1. The front part of a ship; bow, prow
