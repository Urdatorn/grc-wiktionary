Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

From the root of λίσσομαι (líssomai) +‎ -η (-ē).

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /li.tɛ̌ː/ → /liˈti/ → /liˈti/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /li.tɛ̌ː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /liˈte̝/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /liˈti/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /liˈti/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /liˈti/

Noun

[edit]

λῐτή • (litḗ) f (genitive λῐτῆς); first declension

  1. prayer, entreaty
