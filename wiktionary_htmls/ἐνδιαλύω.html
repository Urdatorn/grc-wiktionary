Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

From ἐν- (en-, “in”) +‎ διαλῡ́ω (dialū́ō, “dissolve”)

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /en.di.a.ly̌ː.ɔː/ → /en.di.aˈly.o/ → /en.di.aˈli.o/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /en.di.a.ly̌ː.ɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /en.di.aˈly.o/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /en.di.aˈly.o/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /en.di.aˈly.o/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /en.di.aˈli.o/

Verb

[edit]

ἐνδῐᾰλῡ́ω • (endialū́ō)

  1. to loosen, disperse a clot of blood
