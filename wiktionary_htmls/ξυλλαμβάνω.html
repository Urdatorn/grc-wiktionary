Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /ksyl.lam.bá.nɔː/ → /ksyl.lamˈba.no/ → /ksi.lamˈba.no/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /ksyl.lam.bá.nɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /ksyl.lamˈba.no/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /ksyl.lamˈba.no/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /ksyl.lamˈba.no/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /ksi.lamˈba.no/

Verb

[edit]

ξυλλαμβάνω • (xullambánō)

  1. Old Attic form of συλλαμβάνω (sullambánō)
