Ancient Greek

[edit]

Alternative forms

[edit]
  • ῥείω (rheíō) — Epic

Etymology

[edit]

From Proto-Hellenic *hréyyō, from Proto-Indo-European *sréwyeti, from *srew- (“to flow, stream”).[1] Cognates include Sanskrit स्रवति (srávati), Old Church Slavonic строуꙗ (struja, “stream”).
