Ancient Greek

[edit]

Alternative forms

[edit]
  • ξυναδικέω (xunadikéō) — Old Attic

Etymology

[edit]

From συν- (sun-) +‎ ἀδικέω (adikéō).

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /sy.na.di.ké.ɔː/ → /sy.na.ðiˈce.o/ → /si.na.ðiˈce.o/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /sy.na.di.ké.ɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /sy.na.diˈke.o/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /sy.na.ðiˈce.o/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /sy.na.ðiˈce.o/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /si.na.ðiˈce.o/

Verb

[edit]

σῠνᾰδῐκέω • (sunadikéō)

  1. to join in wrong or injury [with dative ‘with’]
  2. (passive voice) to be wronged alike
