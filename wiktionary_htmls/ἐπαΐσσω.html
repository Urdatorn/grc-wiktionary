Ancient Greek

[edit]

Alternative forms

[edit]
  • ἐπᾴσσω (epā́issō)
  • ἐπᾴττω (epā́ittō), ἐπάττω (epáttō) — Attic

Etymology

[edit]

ἐπι- (epi-) +‎ ᾱ̓ΐσσω (āḯssō)

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /e.paː.ís.sɔː/ → /e.paˈis.so/ → /e.paˈi.so/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /e.paː.ís.sɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /e.paˈis.so/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /e.paˈis.so/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /e.paˈis.so/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /e.paˈi.so/

Verb

[edit]

ἐπᾱΐσσω • (epāḯssō)

  1. to dart or spring at or upon
