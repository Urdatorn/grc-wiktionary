Ancient Greek

[edit]

Alternative forms

[edit]
  • ἀνδέω (andéō) — poetic

Etymology

[edit]

ἀνα- (ana-) +‎ δέω (déō)

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /a.na.dé.ɔː/ → /a.naˈðe.o/ → /a.naˈðe.o/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /a.na.dé.ɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /a.naˈde.o/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /a.naˈðe.o/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /a.naˈðe.o/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /a.naˈðe.o/

Verb

[edit]

ἀναδέω • (anadéō)

  1. to tie up
  2. to crown
