Ancient Greek

[edit]

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /e.pál.lo.mai̯/ → /eˈpal.lo.mɛ/ → /eˈpa.lo.me/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /e.pál.lo.mai̯/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /eˈpal.lo.mɛ/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /eˈpal.lo.mɛ/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /eˈpal.lo.me/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /eˈpa.lo.me/

Verb

[edit]

ἐπάλλομαι • (epállomai)

  1. Epic and Ionic form of ἐφάλλομαι (ephállomai)
