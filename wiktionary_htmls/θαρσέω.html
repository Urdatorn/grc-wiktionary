Ancient Greek

[edit]

Alternative forms

[edit]
  • θαρρέω (tharrhéō) — Attic

Etymology

[edit]

From θάρσος (thársos, “courage”) +‎ -έω (-éō, denominal suffix).

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /tʰar.sé.ɔː/ → /θarˈse.o/ → /θarˈse.o/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /tʰar.sé.ɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /tʰarˈse.o/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /θarˈse.o/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /θarˈse.o/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /θarˈse.o/

Verb

[edit]

θαρσέω • (tharséō)

  1. to dare, to be of courage
