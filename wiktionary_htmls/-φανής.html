Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

From φαν- (phan-), the root of φαίνω (phaínō), + -ής (-ḗs, adjective suffix).

Combining form

[edit]

-φᾰνής • (-phanḗs) m or f (neuter -φᾰνές)

  1. Combining form used in adjectives of appearance
