Ancient Greek

[edit]

Alternative forms

[edit]
  • ζώω (zṓō) — Epic, Ionic
  • ζάω (záō)

Etymology

[edit]

Contracted from ζώω (zṓō), from Proto-Hellenic *ďṓwō, from *gʷyṓwō, from Proto-Indo-European *gʷyéh₃woh₂. Doublet of βῐόω (bióō).[1]

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /zdɔ̂ː/ → /zo/ → /zo/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /zdɔ̂ː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /zo/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /zo/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /zo/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /zo/

Verb

[edit]

ζῶ • (zô)

  1. to live
