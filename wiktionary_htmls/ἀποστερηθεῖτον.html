Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /a.pos.te.rɛː.tʰêː.ton/ → /a.pos.te.riˈθi.ton/ → /a.pos.te.riˈθi.ton/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /a.pos.te.rɛː.tʰêː.ton/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /a.pos.te.re̝ˈtʰi.ton/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /a.pos.te.riˈθi.ton/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /a.pos.te.riˈθi.ton/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /a.pos.te.riˈθi.ton/

Verb

[edit]

ἀποστερηθεῖτον • (aposterētheîton)

  1. second-person dual aorist passive optative of ἀποστερέω (aposteréō)
