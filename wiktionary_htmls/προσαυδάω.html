Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

προσ- (pros-) +‎ αὐδάω (audáō)

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /pro.sau̯.dá.ɔː/ → /pro.saβˈða.o/ → /pro.savˈða.o/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /pro.sau̯.dá.ɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /pro.sawˈda.o/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /pro.saβˈða.o/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /pro.savˈða.o/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /pro.savˈða.o/

Verb

[edit]

προσαυδάω • (prosaudáō)

  1. to speak to, address
  2. to speak of, speak about
