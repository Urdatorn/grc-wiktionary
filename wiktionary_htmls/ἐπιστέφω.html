Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

ἐπι- (epi-) +‎ στέφω (stéphō)

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /e.pis.té.pʰɔː/ → /e.pisˈte.ɸo/ → /e.pisˈte.fo/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /e.pis.té.pʰɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /e.pisˈte.pʰo/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /e.pisˈte.ɸo/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /e.pisˈte.fo/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /e.pisˈte.fo/

Verb

[edit]

ἐπιστέφω • (epistéphō)

  1. to surround with or as with a chaplet
  2. (Epic, in middle) to fill to the brim
  3. to offer (libations as an honour to the dead)
