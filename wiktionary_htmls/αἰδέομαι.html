Ancient Greek

[edit]

Alternative forms

[edit]
  • αἴδομαι (aídomai) — Homeric

Etymology

[edit]

αἰδώς (aidṓs, “shame”) +‎ -έομαι (-éomai)

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /ai̯.dé.o.mai̯/ → /ɛˈðe.o.mɛ/ → /eˈðe.o.me/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /ai̯.dé.o.mai̯/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /ɛˈde.o.mɛ/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /ɛˈðe.o.mɛ/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /eˈðe.o.me/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /eˈðe.o.me/

Verb

[edit]

αἰδέομαι • (aidéomai)

  1. to be ashamed
    Synonym: αἰσχῡ́νομαι (aiskhū́nomai)
  2. to fear
