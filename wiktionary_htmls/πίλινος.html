Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

From πῖλος (pîlos, “felt”) +‎ -ινος (-inos).

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /pí.li.nos/ → /ˈpi.li.nos/ → /ˈpi.li.nos/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /pí.li.nos/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /ˈpi.li.nos/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /ˈpi.li.nos/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /ˈpi.li.nos/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /ˈpi.li.nos/

Adjective

[edit]

πίλῐνος • (pílinos) m (feminine πιλῐ́νη, neuter πίλῐνον); first/second declension

  1. made of felt
