Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

Uncertain. Could be related to σπάω (spáō, “to draw, pull”).

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /as.páz.do.mai̯/ → /asˈpa.zo.mɛ/ → /asˈpa.zo.me/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /as.páz.do.mai̯/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /asˈpa.zo.mɛ/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /asˈpa.zo.mɛ/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /asˈpa.zo.me/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /asˈpa.zo.me/

Verb

[edit]

ἀσπάζομαι • (aspázomai)

  1. to welcome kindly, bid welcome, greet
    • 800 BCE – 600 BCE, Homer, Odyssey 3.35
    • 522 BCE – 443 BCE, Pindar, Isthmian Ode 2.37
    • 460 BCE – 420 BCE, Herodotus, Histories 1.122, (compare 3.1)
    • 460 BCE – 420 BCE, Herodotus, Histories 2.121
    • 460 BCE – 420 BCE, Herodotus, Histories 2.4
    • 458 BCE, Aeschylus, Agamemnon 524
    • 429 BCE, Sophocles, Oedipus the King 596
    • 423 BCE, Aristophanes, The Clouds 1145
    • 388 BCE, Aristophanes, Plutus 1042
    • 380 BCE, Plato, Euthydemus 273B
    • 388 BCE, Aristophanes, Plutus 752
    • 428 BCE – 347 BCE, Plato, Charmides 153B
    • 428 BCE, Euripides, Hippolytus 101, (compare Plato, The Republic 499A)
    • 46 CE – 120 CE, Plutarch, Mark Antony 76
    • 60 BCE – 7 BCE, Dionysius of Halicarnassus, Roman Antiquities 4.39
    • 413 BCE, Euripides, Ion 587
    • 380 BCE, Plato, The Republic 376A
    1. to take leave of
      • 415 BCE, Euripides, The Trojan Women 1276
      • 430 BCE – 354 BCE, Xenophon, Cyropaedia 1.3.2
      • 445 BCE – 380 BCE, Lysias, Collected Works 133.22
  2. to kiss, embrace, caress
    • 422 BCE, Aristophanes, The Wasps 607
    • 46 CE – 120 CE, Plutarch, Romulus 1
    • 430 BCE – 354 BCE, Xenophon, Memorabilia 2.3.9
    • 413 BCE, Euripides, Ion 1363
    • 428 BCE – 347 BCE, Plato, Laws 689A
    • 400 BCE – 387 BCE, Plato, Apology 29D
  3. (of things) to follow eagerly, cleave to
    • 385 BCE – 380 BCE, Plato, Symposium 192A
    • 380 BCE, Plato, The Republic 475A
    • 430 BCE – 354 BCE, Xenophon, Cynegeticus 3.7
  4. to be glad that
    • 388 BCE, Aristophanes, Plutus 324
