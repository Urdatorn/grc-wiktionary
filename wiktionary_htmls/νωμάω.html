Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

From the root of νέμω (némō), from Proto-Indo-European *nem-.

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /nɔː.má.ɔː/ → /noˈma.o/ → /noˈma.o/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /nɔː.má.ɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /noˈma.o/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /noˈma.o/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /noˈma.o/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /noˈma.o/

Verb

[edit]

νωμάω • (nōmáō)

  1. to deal out, distribute
