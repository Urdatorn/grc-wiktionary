Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

ἐπι- (epi-) +‎ ὀνομάζω (onomázō)

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /e.po.no.máz.dɔː/ → /e.po.noˈma.zo/ → /e.po.noˈma.zo/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /e.po.no.máz.dɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /e.po.noˈma.zo/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /e.po.noˈma.zo/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /e.po.noˈma.zo/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /e.po.noˈma.zo/

Verb

[edit]

ἐπονομάζω • (eponomázō)

  1. to name, call (give a name to)
  2. to call by name
