Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

Of Pre-Greek origin.

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /bdál.lɔː/ → /ˈβðal.lo/ → /ˈvða.lo/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /bdál.lɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /ˈbdal.lo/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /ˈβðal.lo/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /ˈvðal.lo/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /ˈvða.lo/

Verb

[edit]

βδάλλω • (bdállō)

  1. to milk cows
    1. (middle voice, of a cow) to yield to milking
  2. to suck
