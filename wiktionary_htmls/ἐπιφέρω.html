Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

From ἐπι- (epi-, “upon”) +‎ φέρω (phérō, “to bring, bear, carry”).

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /e.pi.pʰé.rɔː/ → /e.piˈɸe.ro/ → /e.piˈfe.ro/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /e.pi.pʰé.rɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /e.piˈpʰe.ro/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /e.piˈɸe.ro/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /e.piˈfe.ro/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /e.piˈfe.ro/

Verb

[edit]

ἐπιφέρω • (epiphérō)

  1. to bestow, put, lay upon
