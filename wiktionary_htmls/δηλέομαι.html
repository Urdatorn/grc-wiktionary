Ancient Greek

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /dɛː.lé.o.mai̯/ → /ðiˈle.o.mɛ/ → /ðiˈle.o.me/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /dɛː.lé.o.mai̯/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /de̝ˈle.o.mɛ/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /ðiˈle.o.mɛ/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /ðiˈle.o.me/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /ðiˈle.o.me/

Verb

[edit]

δηλέομαι • (dēléomai)

  1. to hurt, damage, spoil, waste
  2. to destroy
