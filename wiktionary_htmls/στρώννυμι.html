Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

See στόρνῡμι (stórnūmi).[1]

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /strɔ̌ːn.nyː.mi/ → /ˈstron.ny.mi/ → /ˈstro.ni.mi/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /strɔ̌ːn.nyː.mi/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /ˈstron.ny.mi/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /ˈstron.ny.mi/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /ˈstron.ny.mi/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /ˈstro.ni.mi/

Verb

[edit]

στρώννῡμῐ • (strṓnnūmi)

  1. Alternative form of στόρνῡμι (stórnūmi, “to spread, smooth out”)
