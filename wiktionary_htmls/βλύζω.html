Ancient Greek

[edit]

Alternative forms

[edit]
  • βλύω (blúō)
  • βλύσσω (blússō)
  • βλυστάνω (blustánō)

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /blýz.dɔː/ → /ˈβly.zo/ → /ˈvli.zo/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /blýz.dɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /ˈbly.zo/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /ˈβly.zo/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /ˈvly.zo/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /ˈvli.zo/

Verb

[edit]

βλύζω • (blúzō)

  1. (of liquids) to bubble or gush forth
