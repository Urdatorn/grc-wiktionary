Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

ἐμ- (em-) +‎ βάλλω (bállō)

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /em.bál.lɔː/ → /emˈbal.lo/ → /emˈba.lo/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /em.bál.lɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /emˈbal.lo/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /emˈbal.lo/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /emˈbal.lo/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /emˈba.lo/

Verb

[edit]

ἐμβάλλω • (embállō)

  1. to throw in, cast in
  2. to put into (someone's hands)
  3. to bring (to a place)
  4. to infuse with, inspire with
  5. to apply oneself to
