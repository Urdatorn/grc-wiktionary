Ancient Greek

[edit]

Alternative forms

[edit]
  • συνομνύω (sunomnúō)
  • ξυνόμνῡμι (xunómnūmi) — Old Attic

Etymology

[edit]

From συν- (sun-) +‎ ὄμνῡμι (ómnūmi).

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /sy.nóm.nyː.mi/ → /syˈnom.ny.mi/ → /siˈnom.ni.mi/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /sy.nóm.nyː.mi/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /syˈnom.ny.mi/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /syˈnom.ny.mi/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /syˈnom.ny.mi/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /siˈnom.ni.mi/

Verb

[edit]

σῠνόμνῡμῐ • (sunómnūmi)

  1. to swear together
  2. to promise by oath
  3. to join in a league or confederacy
  4. to form a confederacy with others
  5. to conspire
