Ancient Greek

[edit]

Alternative forms

[edit]
  • περῐτᾰ́μνω (peritámnō) — Ionic

Etymology

[edit]

From περῐ- (peri-, “around”) +‎ τέμνω (témnō, “to cut”).

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /pe.ri.tém.nɔː/ → /pe.riˈtem.no/ → /pe.riˈtem.no/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /pe.ri.tém.nɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /pe.riˈtem.no/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /pe.riˈtem.no/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /pe.riˈtem.no/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /pe.riˈtem.no/

Verb

[edit]

περῐτέμνω • (peritémnō)

  1. to cut around
  2. to circumcise
