Ancient Greek

[edit]

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /pruː.pʰěː.lɔː/ → /pruˈɸi.lo/ → /pruˈfi.lo/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /pruː.pʰěː.lɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /pruˈpʰi.lo/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /pruˈɸi.lo/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /pruˈfi.lo/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /pruˈfi.lo/

Verb

[edit]

προὐφείλω • (proupheílō)

  1. Attic form of προοφείλω (proopheílō) (contracted)
