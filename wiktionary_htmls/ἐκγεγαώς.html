Ancient Greek

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /eɡ.ɡe.ɡa.ɔ̌ːs/ → /eɡ.ʝe.ɣaˈos/ → /eɡ.ʝe.ɣaˈos/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /eɡ.ɡe.ɡa.ɔ̌ːs/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /eɡ.ɡe.ɡaˈos/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /eɡ.ʝe.ɣaˈos/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /eɡ.ʝe.ɣaˈos/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /eɡ.ʝe.ɣaˈos/

Participle

[edit]

ἐκγεγᾰώς • (ekgegaṓs) m (feminine ἐκγεγᾰυῖᾰ, neuter ἐκγεγᾰός); first/third declension

  1. perfect active participle of ἐκγίγνομαι (ekgígnomai)
