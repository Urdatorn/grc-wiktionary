Ancient Greek

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /a.kʰný.me.nos/ → /aˈxny.me.nos/ → /aˈxni.me.nos/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /a.kʰný.me.nos/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /aˈkʰny.me.nos/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /aˈxny.me.nos/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /aˈxny.me.nos/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /aˈxni.me.nos/

Participle

[edit]

ἀχνύμενος • (akhnúmenos) m (feminine ἀχνῠμένη, neuter ἀχνύμενον); first/second declension

  1. present mediopassive participle of ἄχνυμαι (ákhnumai)
