Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

προ- (pro-) +‎ βαίνω (baínō)

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /pro.bǎi̯.nɔː/ → /proˈβɛ.no/ → /proˈve.no/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /pro.bǎi̯.nɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /proˈbɛ.no/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /proˈβɛ.no/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /proˈve.no/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /proˈve.no/

Verb

[edit]

προβαίνω • (probaínō)

  1. to step on, step forward, advance
  2. to advance, proceed
  3. to go before
  4. to overstep
