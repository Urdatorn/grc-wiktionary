Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

κατα- (kata-) +‎ νοέω (noéō)

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /ka.ta.no.é.ɔː/ → /ka.ta.noˈe.o/ → /ka.ta.noˈe.o/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /ka.ta.no.é.ɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /ka.ta.noˈe.o/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /ka.ta.noˈe.o/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /ka.ta.noˈe.o/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /ka.ta.noˈe.o/

Verb

[edit]

κᾰτᾰνοέω • (katanoéō)

  1. to perceive, observe, watch
  2. to learn
  3. to take to heart, consider
