Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

ἀντι- (anti-) +‎ πολεμέω (poleméō)

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /an.ti.po.le.mé.ɔː/ → /an.ti.po.leˈme.o/ → /an.di.po.leˈme.o/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /an.ti.po.le.mé.ɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /an.ti.po.leˈme.o/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /an.ti.po.leˈme.o/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /an.ti.po.leˈme.o/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /an.di.po.leˈme.o/

Pronunciation

[edit]

ἀντῐπολεμέω • (antipoleméō)

  1. to wage war against
