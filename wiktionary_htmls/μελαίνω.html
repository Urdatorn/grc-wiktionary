Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

From Proto-Indo-European *mélh₂nyeti. Surface analysis: μέλᾱς (mélās) +‎ -ι̯ω (-i̯ō).

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /me.lǎi̯.nɔː/ → /meˈlɛ.no/ → /meˈle.no/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /me.lǎi̯.nɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /meˈlɛ.no/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /meˈlɛ.no/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /meˈle.no/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /meˈle.no/

Verb

[edit]

μελαίνω • (melaínō)

  1. to blacken, render black
