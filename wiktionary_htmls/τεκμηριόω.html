Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

τεκμήριον (tekmḗrion, “proof”) +‎ -όω (-óō)

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /tek.mɛː.ri.ó.ɔː/ → /tek.mi.riˈo.o/ → /tek.mi.riˈo.o/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /tek.mɛː.ri.ó.ɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /tek.me̝.riˈo.o/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /tek.mi.riˈo.o/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /tek.mi.riˈo.o/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /tek.mi.riˈo.o/

Verb

[edit]

τεκμηρῐόω • (tekmērióō)

  1. to prove positively
