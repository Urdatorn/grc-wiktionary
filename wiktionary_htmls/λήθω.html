Ancient Greek

[edit]

Alternative forms

[edit]
  • λᾱ́θω (lā́thō) — Doric

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /lɛ̌ː.tʰɔː/ → /ˈli.θo/ → /ˈli.θo/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /lɛ̌ː.tʰɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /ˈle̝.tʰo/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /ˈli.θo/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /ˈli.θo/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /ˈli.θo/

Verb

[edit]

λήθω • (lḗthō)

  1. Alternative form of λανθάνω (lanthánō) used especially in compounds. (Ionic)
