Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

ἐπι- (epi-) +‎ στρατεύω (strateúō)

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /e.pis.tra.těu̯.ɔː/ → /e.pis.traˈte.βo/ → /e.pis.traˈte.vo/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /e.pis.tra.těu̯.ɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /e.pis.traˈte.wo/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /e.pis.traˈte.βo/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /e.pis.traˈte.vo/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /e.pis.traˈte.vo/

Verb

[edit]

ἐπιστρᾰτεύω • (epistrateúō)

  1. to wage war on, march against
