Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

From ἐπι- (epi-) +‎ ἀκούω (akoúō).

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /e.pa.kǔː.ɔː/ → /e.paˈku.o/ → /e.paˈku.o/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /e.pa.kǔː.ɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /e.paˈku.o/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /e.paˈku.o/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /e.paˈku.o/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /e.paˈku.o/

Verb

[edit]

ἐπᾰκούω • (epakoúō)

  1. (transitive)to listen to, hearken to, hear
  2. (intransitive) to give ear, listen
  3. to obey [with genitive]
