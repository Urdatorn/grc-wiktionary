Ancient Greek

[edit]

Alternative forms

[edit]
  • καταθνήσκω (katathnḗskō)
  • κατθναίσκω (katthnaískō), καταθνάσκω (katathnáskō) — Aeolic

Etymology

[edit]

κατα- (kata-) +‎ θνῄσκω (thnḗiskō)

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /ka.ta.tʰnɛ̌ːi̯s.kɔː/ → /ka.taˈθnis.ko/ → /ka.taˈθnis.ko/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /ka.ta.tʰnɛ̌ːi̯s.kɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /ka.taˈtʰne̝s.ko/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /ka.taˈθnis.ko/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /ka.taˈθnis.ko/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /ka.taˈθnis.ko/

Verb

[edit]

κᾰταθνῄσκω • (katathnḗiskō)

  1. to die
  2. (in perfect) to be dead
