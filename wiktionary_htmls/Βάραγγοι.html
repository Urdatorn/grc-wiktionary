Ancient Greek

[edit]

Alternative forms

[edit]
  • Φάργανοι (Phárganoi)

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /ˈva.raɲ.ɟi/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /ˈva.raɲ.ɟy/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /ˈva.raɲ.ɟi/

Proper noun

[edit]

Βάραγγοι • (Bárangoi) m (Byzantine)

  1. nominative/vocative plural of Βάραγγος (Bárangos, “Varangian”)

Proper noun

[edit]

Βάραγγοι • (Bárangoi) m pl (genitive Βαράγγων); second declension (Byzantine)

  1. (historical, collective) the Varangians (a medieval tribe or ethnic grouping of Norse origin)

Greek

[edit]

Pronunciation

[edit]
  • IPA(key): /ˈva.ɾaŋ.ɟi/
  • Hyphenation: Βά‧ραγ‧γοι

Proper noun

[edit]

Βάραγγοι • (Várangoi) m

  1. (historical, collective) the Varangians (a medieval tribe or ethnic grouping of Norse origin)
  2. Nominative and vocative plural form of Βάραγγος (Várangos, “Varangian”).
