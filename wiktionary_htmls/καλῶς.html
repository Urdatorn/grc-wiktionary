Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

Adverb from καλός (kalós, “beautiful”) +‎ -ως (-ōs).

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /ka.lɔ̂ːs/ → /kaˈlos/ → /kaˈlos/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /ka.lɔ̂ːs/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /kaˈlos/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /kaˈlos/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /kaˈlos/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /kaˈlos/

Adverb

[edit]

καλῶς • (kalôs) (comparative κάλλιον)

  1. adverb of καλός (kalós)

Descendants

[edit]
  • Greek: καλώς (kalós)
  • Tsakonian: καούρ (kaoúr)
