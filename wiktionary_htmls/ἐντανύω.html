Ancient Greek

[edit]

Alternative forms

[edit]
  • ἐντείνω (enteínō)

Etymology

[edit]

From ἐν- (en-) +‎ τανύω (tanúō).

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /en.ta.ný.ɔː/ → /en.taˈny.o/ → /en.daˈni.o/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /en.ta.ný.ɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /en.taˈny.o/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /en.taˈny.o/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /en.taˈny.o/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /en.daˈni.o/

Verb

[edit]

ἐντᾰνύω • (entanúō) (poetic, Epic, Ionic)

  1. to stretch tight
  2. (archery) to string (a bow)
