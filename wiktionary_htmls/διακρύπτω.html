Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

From δια- (dia-, “through”) +‎ κρύπτω (krúptō, “hide”)

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /di.a.krýp.tɔː/ → /ði.aˈkryp.to/ → /ði.aˈkrip.to/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /di.a.krýp.tɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /di.aˈkryp.to/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /ði.aˈkryp.to/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /ði.aˈkryp.to/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /ði.aˈkrip.to/

Verb

[edit]

δῐᾰκρύπτω • (diakrúptō)

  1. to hide, conceal, disguise
  2. to bury
