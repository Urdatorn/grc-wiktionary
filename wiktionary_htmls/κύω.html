Ancient Greek

[edit]

Alternative forms

[edit]
  • κυέω (kuéō)

Etymology

[edit]

From Proto-Indo-European *ḱewh₁- (“to swell”). Cognate with κύριος (kúrios), Latin cumulus, cavus.

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /ký.ɔː/ → /ˈcy.o/ → /ˈci.o/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /ký.ɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /ˈky.o/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /ˈcy.o/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /ˈcy.o/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /ˈci.o/

Verb

[edit]

κῠ́ω • (kúō)

  1. (present and imperfect) to conceive, become pregnant
    1. (rarely with accusative) to become pregnant with...
  2. (aorist) to impregnate
