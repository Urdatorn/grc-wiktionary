Ancient Greek

[edit]

Alternative forms

[edit]
  • πᾰ́λῐ (páli)

Etymology

[edit]

Frozen adverbial accusative of *πάλις (*pális) from Proto-Indo-European *kʷl̥His, from *kʷel- (“to revolve”) (whence πέλω (pélō, “to go, be”) and πάλαι (pálai, “before, long ago”)).[1]
