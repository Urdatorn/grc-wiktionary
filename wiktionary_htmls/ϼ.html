Translingual

[edit]

Letter

[edit]

ϼ

  1. Used as shorthand for words beginning with rho.
  2. A graphic substitute for rotated eth, equivalent to IPA ⟨ð̞⟩.

Ancient Greek

[edit]

Noun

[edit]

ϼ • (r.)

  1. Abbreviation of ν μναῖ (n mnaî, “50 mina”).
    • 3rd c. AD, Epiphanius of Salamis, On weights and measures Weights and Measures:
      … τὴν δὲ μνᾶν τῷ μ ἐπικείμενον ἔχοντι τὸ ν (μν) (τὸ ν μν πεντήκοντα μνᾶς, ἐνίοτε δὲ ρ διὰ μέσου γραμμὴν ἔχον τὸ αὐτὸ σημαίνει ϼ) …
      and the mina by the μ having the ν on top of it (μν) (50 μν = fifty mina, and sometimes ρ having a line through the middle means the same ϼ) …
