Ancient Greek

[edit]

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /pai̯.deu̯.só.me.nos/ → /pɛ.ðeɸˈso.me.nos/ → /pe.ðefˈso.me.nos/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /pai̯.deu̯.só.me.nos/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /pɛ.dewˈso.me.nos/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /pɛ.ðeɸˈso.me.nos/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /pe.ðefˈso.me.nos/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /pe.ðefˈso.me.nos/

Participle

[edit]

παιδευσόμενος • (paideusómenos) m (feminine παιδευσομένη, neuter παιδευσόμενον); first/second declension

  1. future middle participle of παιδεύω (paideúō)
