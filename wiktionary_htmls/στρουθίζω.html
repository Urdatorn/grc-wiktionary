Ancient Greek

[edit]

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /struː.tʰíz.dɔː/ → /struˈθi.zo/ → /struˈθi.zo/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /struː.tʰíz.dɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /struˈtʰi.zo/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /struˈθi.zo/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /struˈθi.zo/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /struˈθi.zo/

Etymology 1

[edit]

From στρουθός (strouthós, “house sparrow”) +‎ -ίζω (-ízō).

Verb

[edit]

στρουθῐ́ζω • (strouthízō)

  1. to chirp like a sparrow, twitter

Etymology 2

[edit]

From στρούθῐον (stroúthion, “soapwort”) +‎ -ίζω (-ízō).

Verb

[edit]

στρουθῐ́ζω • (strouthízō)

  1. to cleanse with soapwort
