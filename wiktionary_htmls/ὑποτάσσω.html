Ancient Greek

[edit]

Alternative forms

[edit]
  • ῠ̔ποτᾰ́ττω (hupotáttō) — Attic

Etymology

[edit]

From ῠ̔πο- (hupo-, “under”) +‎ τᾰ́σσω (tássō, “to place”).

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /hy.po.tás.sɔː/ → /y.poˈtas.so/ → /i.poˈta.so/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /hy.po.tás.sɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /(h)y.poˈtas.so/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /y.poˈtas.so/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /y.poˈtas.so/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /i.poˈta.so/

Verb

[edit]

ῠ̔ποτᾰ́σσω • (hupotássō)

  1. to place or arrange under
    • 200 BCE – 118 BCE, Polybius, The Histories 3.36.7
    • 46 CE – 120 CE, Plutarch, Nicias 23
    • 200 BCE – 118 BCE, Polybius, The Histories 18.15.4
    • 460 BCE – 370 BCE, Hippocrates, In the Surgery 16
  2. to post under or behind
    • 125 CE – 200 CE, Lucian, The Parasite: Parasitic an Art 49
    • 86 CE – 160 CE, Arrian, Tactics 26.7
    1. to subject, subdue, make subject
      • 110 BCE – 35 BCE, Philodemus, On Rhetoric 2.204
      • 46 CE – 120 CE, Plutarch, Pompey 64
      • 170 CE – 240 CE, Herodian, History of the Empire from the Death of Marcus 7.2.9
      • New Testament, Epistle to the Philippians 3:21
      • New Testament, Epistle to the Ephesians 1:22
      • New Testament, Epistle to the Colossians 3:18
      • New Testament, Epistle of James 4:7
      • Cyranus, Collected Works 15
      • 170 CE – 240 CE, Herodian, History of the Empire from the Death of Marcus 2.2.8
      • 1 CE – 100 CE, Onasander, Strategikos 1.17
      • 430-530 BC, Phrynichus Comicus, Collected Works 59
      • 200 BCE – 118 BCE, Polybius, The Histories 3.13.8
      • 110 BCE – 35 BCE, Philodemus, On Household Management 72
      • 108 CE, Arrian, Discourses of Epictetus 4.4.33
      • Stoicorum Veterum Fragmenta 3.64
  3. to put after
    • 46 CE – 120 CE, Plutarch, Moralia 2.1020A
    • 90 CE – 168 CE, Ptolemy, Geography 6.7.27
    1. (logic) to take as a minor premise
      • 108 CE, Arrian, Discourses of Epictetus 4.1.161
  4. to govern the subjunctive
    • Etymologicum Magnum 471.16
