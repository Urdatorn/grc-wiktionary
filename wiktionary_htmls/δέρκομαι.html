Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

From Proto-Hellenic *dérkomai, from Proto-Indo-European *derḱ-. Cognate with Sanskrit दृश् (dṛś, “see”).

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /dér.ko.mai̯/ → /ˈðer.ko.mɛ/ → /ˈðer.ko.me/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /dér.ko.mai̯/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /ˈder.ko.mɛ/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /ˈðer.ko.mɛ/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /ˈðer.ko.me/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /ˈðer.ko.me/

Verb

[edit]

δέρκομαι • (dérkomai)

  1. to see, see clearly
  2. to watch
  3. to live
  4. to flash, gleam
