Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

From κατα- (kata-, “intensifying prefix”) +‎ οικονομέω (oikonoméō, “manage as a steward”)

Verb

[edit]

κατοικονομέω • (katoikonoméō)

  1. manage well, be well-managed
