Ancient Greek

[edit]

Alternative forms

[edit]
  • βάρβιτον (bárbiton)
  • βάρμιτος (bármitos)
  • βάρμος (bármos)
  • βάρωμος (bárōmos)

Etymology

[edit]

Uncertain, probably from Phrygian.

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /bár.bi.tos/ → /ˈβar.βi.tos/ → /ˈvar.vi.tos/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /bár.bi.tos/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /ˈbar.bi.tos/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /ˈβar.βi.tos/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /ˈvar.vi.tos/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /ˈvar.vi.tos/

Noun

[edit]

βάρβῐτος • (bárbitos) m or f (genitive βαρβῐ́του); second declension

  1. (music) barbitos
