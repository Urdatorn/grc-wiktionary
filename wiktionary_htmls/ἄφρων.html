Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

ἀ- (a-, “not”) +‎ -φρων (-phrōn, “mind”)

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /á.pʰrɔːn/ → /ˈa.ɸron/ → /ˈa.fron/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /á.pʰrɔːn/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /ˈa.pʰron/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /ˈa.ɸron/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /ˈa.fron/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /ˈa.fron/

Adjective

[edit]

ἄφρων • (áphrōn) m or f (neuter ἄφρον); third declension

  1. senseless, crazed, frantic, silly, foolish
