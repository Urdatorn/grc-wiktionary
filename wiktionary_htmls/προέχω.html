Ancient Greek

[edit]

Alternative forms

[edit]
  • προὔχω (proúkhō)

Etymology

[edit]

προ- (pro-) +‎ ἔχω (ékhō)

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /pro.é.kʰɔː/ → /proˈe.xo/ → /proˈe.xo/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /pro.é.kʰɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /proˈe.kʰo/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /proˈe.xo/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /proˈe.xo/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /proˈe.xo/

Verb

[edit]

προέχω • (proékhō)

  1. to be ahead
  2. to jut forward
  3. (in middle) to hold or have before oneself
  4. (in middle) to excel, be better
