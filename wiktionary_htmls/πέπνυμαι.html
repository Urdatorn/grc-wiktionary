Ancient Greek

[edit]

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /pé.pnyː.mai̯/ → /ˈpe.pny.mɛ/ → /ˈpe.pni.me/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /pé.pnyː.mai̯/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /ˈpe.pny.mɛ/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /ˈpe.pny.mɛ/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /ˈpe.pny.me/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /ˈpe.pni.me/

Verb

[edit]

πέπνῡμαι • (pépnūmai)

  1. first-person singular perfect mediopassive indicative of πνέω (pnéō)

Verb

[edit]

πέπνῡμαι • (pépnūmai)

  1. to be conscious
  2. to be wise
  3. to breathe
