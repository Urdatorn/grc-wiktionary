Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

From παρα- (para-) +‎ βοηθέω (boēthéō).

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /pa.ra.bo.ɛː.tʰé.ɔː/ → /pa.ra.βo.iˈθe.o/ → /pa.ra.vo.iˈθe.o/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /pa.ra.bo.ɛː.tʰé.ɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /pa.ra.bo.e̝ˈtʰe.o/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /pa.ra.βo.iˈθe.o/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /pa.ra.vo.iˈθe.o/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /pa.ra.vo.iˈθe.o/

Verb

[edit]

πᾰρᾰβοηθέω • (paraboēthéō)

  1. to come to aid, come to the rescue (of)
