Ancient Greek

[edit]

Alternative forms

[edit]
  • ἀμπαύω (ampaúō) — Ionic

Etymology

[edit]

From ἀνα- (ana-, “thorough”) +‎ παύω (paúō, “stop, end”).

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /a.na.pǎu̯.ɔː/ → /a.naˈpa.βo/ → /a.naˈpa.vo/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /a.na.pǎu̯.ɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /a.naˈpa.wo/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /a.naˈpa.βo/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /a.naˈpa.vo/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /a.naˈpa.vo/

Verb

[edit]

ἀναπαύω • (anapaúō)

  1. to cause to rest, to soothe, refresh
