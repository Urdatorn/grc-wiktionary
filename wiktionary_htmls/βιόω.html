Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

βίος (bíos) +‎ -όω (-óō). Doublet of inherited ζῶ (zô).

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /bi.ó.ɔː/ → /βiˈo.o/ → /viˈo.o/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /bi.ó.ɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /biˈo.o/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /βiˈo.o/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /viˈo.o/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /viˈo.o/

Verb

[edit]

βῐόω • (bióō)

  1. to live
