Ancient Greek

[edit]

Alternative forms

[edit]
  • χᾰλκοῦς (khalkoûs) — Attic
  • χᾰ́λκειος (khálkeios), χᾰλκήιος (khalkḗios) — Epic

Etymology

[edit]

From χᾰλκός (khalkós, “copper”) +‎ -εος (-eos).

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /kʰál.ke.os/ → /ˈxal.ce.os/ → /ˈxal.ce.os/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /kʰál.ke.os/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /ˈkʰal.ke.os/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /ˈxal.ce.os/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /ˈxal.ce.os/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /ˈxal.ce.os/

Adjective

[edit]

χᾰ́λκεος • (khálkeos) m (feminine χᾰλκέᾱ, neuter χᾰ́λκεον); first/second declension

  1. of copper or bronze, brazen
    • 800 BCE – 600 BCE, Homer, Iliad 8.15
    • 800 BCE – 600 BCE, Homer, Iliad 18.371
    • 800 BCE – 600 BCE, Homer, Odyssey 10.4
    • 800 BCE – 600 BCE, Homer, Iliad 13.30
    • 800 BCE – 600 BCE, Homer, Iliad 5.723
    • 389 BCE – 314 BCE, Aeschines, Collected Works 3.84
    • 800 BCE – 600 BCE, Homer, Iliad 3.317
    • 800 BCE – 600 BCE, Homer, Iliad 3.335
    • 800 BCE – 600 BCE, Homer, Iliad 7.220
    • 800 BCE – 600 BCE, Homer, Iliad 13.398
    • 800 BCE – 600 BCE, Homer, Iliad 13.440
    • 800 BCE – 600 BCE, Homer, Iliad 18.331
    • 408 BCE, Euripides, The Phoenician Women 1359
    • 458 BCE, Aeschylus, The Libation Bearers 686
    • 480 BCE – 406 BCE, Euripides, Cyclops 392
    • 406 BCE, Sophocles, Oedipus at Colonus 1591
    • 497 BCE – 405 BCE, Sophocles, Electra 711
    • 497 BCE – 405 BCE, Sophocles, Women of Trachis 683
    1. (of statues)
      • 460 BCE – 420 BCE, Herodotus, Histories 9.81
      • 522 BCE – 443 BCE, Pindar, Pythian Ode 1.95
      • 343 BCE, Demosthenes, On the False Embassy 272
      • 384 BCE – 322 BCE, Demosthenes, On Organization 21
      • 384 BCE – 322 BCE, Aristotle, Rhetoric 1410A.33
      • 440 BCE – 390 BCE, Andocides, On the Mysteries 38
    2. (χἀλκεος ἀγών) a contest for a shield of brass
      • 522 BCE – 443 BCE, Pindar, Nemean Ode 10.22
  2. (figuratively) brazen, hard, stout, strong
    • 800 BCE – 600 BCE, Homer, Iliad 5.704
    • 460 BCE – 420 BCE, Herodotus, Histories 2.152
    • 46 CE – 120 CE, Plutarch, Demosthenes 11
    • 522 BCE – 443 BCE, Pindar, Isthmian Ode 8.27
    • 800 BCE – 600 BCE, Homer, Iliad 2.490
    • 800 BCE – 600 BCE, Homer, Iliad 18.222
    • 800 BCE – 600 BCE, Homer, Iliad 11.241
    • 413 BCE, Euripides, Ion 1
