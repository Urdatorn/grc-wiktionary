Ancient Greek

[edit]

Alternative forms

[edit]
  • ξυμπολεμέω (xumpoleméō) — Old Attic

Etymology

[edit]

συμ- (sum-) +‎ πολεμέω (poleméō)

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /sym.po.le.mé.ɔː/ → /sym.po.leˈme.o/ → /sim.bo.leˈme.o/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /sym.po.le.mé.ɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /sym.po.leˈme.o/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /sym.po.leˈme.o/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /sym.po.leˈme.o/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /sim.bo.leˈme.o/

Verb

[edit]

συμπολεμέω • (sumpoleméō)

  1. to join in war
    • 460 BCE – 395 BCE, Thucydides, History of the Peloponnesian War 1.18
