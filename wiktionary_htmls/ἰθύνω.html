Ancient Greek

[edit]

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /iː.tʰy̌ː.nɔː/ → /iˈθy.no/ → /iˈθi.no/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /iː.tʰy̌ː.nɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /iˈtʰy.no/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /iˈθy.no/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /iˈθy.no/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /iˈθi.no/

Verb

[edit]

ῑ̓θῡ́νω • (īthū́nō)

  1. Alternative form of εὐθύνω (euthúnō, “to straighten”)
    • 100 CE – 300 CE, Dionysius Periegetes, Periegesis 341
    • 460 BCE – 370 BCE, Hippocrates, On Fractures 7
    • 800 BCE – 600 BCE, Homer, Iliad 16.475
    • 800 BCE – 600 BCE, Homer, Odyssey 23.197
    • 300 CE – 400 CE, Quintus Smyrnaeus, Posthomerica 1.273
    • 300 CE – 400 CE, Quintus Smyrnaeus, Posthomerica 14.500
