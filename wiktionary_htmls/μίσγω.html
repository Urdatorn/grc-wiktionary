Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

From Proto-Indo-European *miǵ-sḱeti, from *meyḱ-, *meyǵ-.

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /míz.ɡɔː/ → /ˈmiz.ɣo/ → /ˈmiz.ɣo/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /míz.ɡɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /ˈmiz.ɡo/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /ˈmiz.ɣo/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /ˈmiz.ɣo/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /ˈmiz.ɣo/

Verb

[edit]

μίσγω • (mísgō)

  1. Synonym of μίγνῡμι (mígnūmi, “to mix”)
