Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

From ἡδύς (hēdús, “sweet”).

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /hɛː.dý.nɔː/ → /iˈðy.no/ → /iˈði.no/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /hɛː.dý.nɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /(h)e̝ˈdy.no/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /iˈðy.no/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /iˈðy.no/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /iˈði.no/

Verb

[edit]

ἡδῠ́νω • (hēdúnō)

  1. to season a dish
  2. (figuratively) to make pleasant
  3. to delight, coax, gratify
