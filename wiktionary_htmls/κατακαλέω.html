Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

κατα- (kata-) +‎ καλέω (kaléō)

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /ka.ta.ka.lé.ɔː/ → /ka.ta.kaˈle.o/ → /ka.ta.kaˈle.o/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /ka.ta.ka.lé.ɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /ka.ta.kaˈle.o/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /ka.ta.kaˈle.o/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /ka.ta.kaˈle.o/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /ka.ta.kaˈle.o/

Verb

[edit]

κᾰτᾰκᾰλέω • (katakaléō)

  1. to call down, summon, invite
