Ancient Greek

[edit]

Alternative forms

[edit]
  • ἐσγράφω (esgráphō) — Ionic, Old Attic

Etymology

[edit]

From εἰσ- (eis-) +‎ γράφω (gráphō).

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /eːz.ɡrá.pʰɔː/ → /izˈɣra.ɸo/ → /izˈɣra.fo/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /eːz.ɡrá.pʰɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /izˈɡra.pʰo/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /izˈɣra.ɸo/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /izˈɣra.fo/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /izˈɣra.fo/

Verb

[edit]

εἰσγράφω • (eisgráphō)

  1. to write in, inscribe
