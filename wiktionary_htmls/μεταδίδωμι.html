Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

From μετα- (meta-) +‎ δίδωμι (dídōmi).

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /me.ta.dí.dɔː.mi/ → /me.taˈði.ðo.mi/ → /me.taˈði.ðo.mi/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /me.ta.dí.dɔː.mi/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /me.taˈdi.do.mi/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /me.taˈði.ðo.mi/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /me.taˈði.ðo.mi/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /me.taˈði.ðo.mi/

Verb

[edit]

μετᾰδίδωμῐ • (metadídōmi)

  1. to give part of, give a share of [with genitive]
  2. to impart
