Ancient Greek

[edit]

Alternative forms

[edit]
  • ἐμπίπλημι (empíplēmi)

Etymology

[edit]

ἐν- (en-) +‎ πίμπλημι (pímplēmi)

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /em.pím.plɛː.mi/ → /emˈpim.pli.mi/ → /emˈbim.bli.mi/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /em.pím.plɛː.mi/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /emˈpim.ple̝.mi/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /emˈpim.pli.mi/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /emˈpim.pli.mi/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /emˈbim.bli.mi/

Verb

[edit]

ἐμπίμπλημι • (empímplēmi)

  1. to fill full
  2. to fulfill
