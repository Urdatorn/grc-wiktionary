Ancient Greek

[edit]

Alternative forms

[edit]
  • ἐπάλλομαι (epállomai) — Epic

Etymology

[edit]

ἐπι- (epi-) +‎ ἅλλομαι (hállomai)

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /e.pʰál.lo.mai̯/ → /eˈɸal.lo.mɛ/ → /eˈfa.lo.me/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /e.pʰál.lo.mai̯/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /eˈpʰal.lo.mɛ/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /eˈɸal.lo.mɛ/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /eˈfal.lo.me/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /eˈfa.lo.me/

Verb

[edit]

ἐφάλλομαι • (ephállomai)

  1. to jump at or on
