  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /ˈe.vra.çe/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /ˈe.vra.çe/

Verb

[edit]

ἔβρᾰχε • (ébrakhe)

  1. to rattle, creak

Usage notes

[edit]

Attested only in the aorist active. The infinitive βρᾰχεῖν (brakheîn) is attested in Hesychius; otherwise only the third-person singular indicative (ἔ)βρᾰχε ((é)brakhe) is attested.
