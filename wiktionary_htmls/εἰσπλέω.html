Ancient Greek

[edit]

Alternative forms

[edit]
  • ἐσπλέω (espléō) — Ionic, Old Attic

Etymology

[edit]

εἰσ- (eis-) +‎ πλέω (pléō)

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /eːs.plé.ɔː/ → /isˈple.o/ → /isˈple.o/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /eːs.plé.ɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /isˈple.o/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /isˈple.o/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /isˈple.o/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /isˈple.o/

Verb

[edit]

εἰσπλέω • (eispléō)

  1. to sail into
