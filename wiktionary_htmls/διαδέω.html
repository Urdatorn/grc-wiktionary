Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

From δια- (dia-) +‎ δέω (déō)

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /di.a.dé.ɔː/ → /ði.aˈðe.o/ → /ði.aˈðe.o/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /di.a.dé.ɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /di.aˈde.o/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /ði.aˈðe.o/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /ði.aˈðe.o/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /ði.aˈðe.o/

Verb

[edit]

δῐᾰδέω • (diadéō)

  1. to bind round
  2. to bind fast
