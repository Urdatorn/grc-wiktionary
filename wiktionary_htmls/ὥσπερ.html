Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

From ὡς (hōs, “as”) + περ (per, enclitic).

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /hɔ̌ːs.per/ → /ˈos.per/ → /ˈos.per/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /hɔ̌ːs.per/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /ˈ(h)os.per/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /ˈos.per/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /ˈos.per/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /ˈos.per/
