Ancient Greek

[edit]

Alternative forms

[edit]
  • ὑπᾰλλᾰ́ττω (hupalláttō) — Attic

Etymology

[edit]

From ὑπο- (hupo-, under) +‎ ἀλλάσσω (allássō, “to change, alter”).

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /hy.pal.lás.sɔː/ → /y.palˈlas.so/ → /i.paˈla.so/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /hy.pal.lás.sɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /(h)y.palˈlas.so/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /y.palˈlas.so/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /y.palˈlas.so/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /i.paˈla.so/

Verb

[edit]

ὑπᾰλλᾰ́σσω • (hupallássō)

  1. to exchange, barter
  2. to change a little
  3. to mortgage
  4. to change gradually
