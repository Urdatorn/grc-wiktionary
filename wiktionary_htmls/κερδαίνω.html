Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

From κέρδος (kérdos) +‎ -αίνω (-aínō).

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /ker.dǎi̯.nɔː/ → /cerˈðɛ.no/ → /cerˈðe.no/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /ker.dǎi̯.nɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /kerˈdɛ.no/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /cerˈðɛ.no/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /cerˈðe.no/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /cerˈðe.no/

Verb

[edit]

κερδαίνω • (kerdaínō)

  1. to gain as profit
