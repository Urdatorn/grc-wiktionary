Ancient Greek

[edit]

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /hy.po.dɛː.má.toi̯n/ → /y.po.ðiˈma.tyn/ → /i.po.ðiˈma.tin/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /hy.po.dɛː.má.toi̯n/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /(h)y.po.de̝ˈma.tyn/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /y.po.ðiˈma.tyn/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /y.po.ðiˈma.tyn/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /i.po.ðiˈma.tin/

Noun

[edit]

ῠ̔ποδημᾰ́τοιν • (hupodēmátoin) n

  1. genitive/dative dual of ῠ̔πόδημᾰ (hupódēma)
