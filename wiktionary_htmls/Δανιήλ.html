Alternative forms

[edit]
  • Δανίηλος (Daníēlos)

Etymology

[edit]

Borrowed from Biblical Hebrew דָּנִיֵּאל (Dāniyyêl).

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /daː.niː.ɛ̌ːl/ → /ða.niˈil/ → /ða.niˈil/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /daː.niː.ɛ̌ːl/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /da.niˈe̝l/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /ða.niˈil/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /ða.niˈil/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /ða.niˈil/

Proper noun

[edit]

Δᾱνῑήλ • (Dānīḗl) m (indeclinable)

  1. a male given name from Biblical Hebrew, equivalent to English Daniel

Descendants

[edit]

  • → Gothic: 𐌳𐌰𐌽𐌹𐌴𐌻 (daniēl)

Greek
