Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

From the root of δαίω (daíō, “to divide, share”).

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /da.té.o.mai̯/ → /ðaˈte.o.mɛ/ → /ðaˈte.o.me/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /da.té.o.mai̯/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /daˈte.o.mɛ/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /ðaˈte.o.mɛ/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /ðaˈte.o.me/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /ðaˈte.o.me/

Verb

[edit]

δᾰτέομαι • (datéomai)

  1. to divide into portions
    Synonym: δαίω (daíō)
