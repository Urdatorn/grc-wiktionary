Ancient Greek

[edit]

Alternative forms

[edit]
  • νεικείω (neikeíō)

Etymology

[edit]

From νεῖκος (neîkos) +‎ -έω (-éō).

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /neː.ké.ɔː/ → /niˈce.o/ → /niˈce.o/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /neː.ké.ɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /niˈke.o/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /niˈce.o/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /niˈce.o/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /niˈce.o/

Verb

[edit]

νεικέω • (neikéō)

  1. to argue, quarrel
