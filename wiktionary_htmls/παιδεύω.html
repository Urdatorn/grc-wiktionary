Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

    From παῖς (paîs, “child”) +‎ -εύω (-eúō).

    Greek

    [edit]

    Etymology

    [edit]

    From Ancient Greek παιδεύω (paideúō, “raise; train; chasten”).

    Pronunciation

    [edit]
    • IPA(key): /peˈðe.vo/
    • Hyphenation: παι‧δεύ‧ω

    Verb

    [edit]

    παιδεύω • (paidévo) (past παίδεψα, passive παιδεύομαι)

    1. to harass, pester, chasten
    2. to torture
    3. (dated) to train
