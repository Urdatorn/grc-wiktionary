Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

From προσ- (pros-, “to, towards, with”) +‎ καλέω (kaléō, “to call”).

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /pros.ka.lé.ɔː/ → /pros.kaˈle.o/ → /pros.kaˈle.o/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /pros.ka.lé.ɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /pros.kaˈle.o/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /pros.kaˈle.o/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /pros.kaˈle.o/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /pros.kaˈle.o/

Verb

[edit]

προσκᾰλέω • (proskaléō)

  1. to call to, call on, summon
    • 460 BCE – 395 BCE, Thucydides, History of the Peloponnesian War 8.98
    • 497 BCE – 405 BCE, Sophocles, Ajax 89
    • 386 BCE – 367 BCE, Plato, Meno 82A
    • 155 CE – 235 CE, Cassius Dio, Collected Works 71.34
  2. (middle voice, perfect passive) to call to oneself, call to one
    • 430 BCE – 354 BCE, Xenophon, Anabasis 7.7.2
    • 125 CE – 200 CE, Lucian, Dialogues of the Gods 19.1
    • 330 BCE, Demosthenes, On the Crown 166
    • 46 CE – 120 CE, Plutarch, Demosthenes 21
    • New Testament, Acts of the Apostles 13:2
    1. (in Attic, of an accuser) to cite or summon to court
      • 422 BCE, Aristophanes, The Wasps 1334
      • 422 BCE, Aristophanes, The Wasps 1417
      • 445 BCE – 380 BCE, Lysias, Against Andocides 11
      • 445 BCE – 380 BCE, Lysias, Defence against a Charge of Taking Bribes 19
      • 330 BCE, Demosthenes, On the Crown 150
      • 445 BCE – 380 BCE, Lysias, Against Pancleon 2
      • 422 BCE, Aristophanes, The Wasps 1406
      • 430 BCE – 354 BCE, Xenophon, Memorabilia 2.9.5
      • 384 BCE – 322 BCE, Demosthenes, Against Macartatus 7
      • 125 CE – 200 CE, Lucian, Timon 46
      • 125 CE – 200 CE, Lucian, The Fisherman 39
      • 384 BCE – 322 BCE, Demosthenes, Against Boeotus 1 17
      • 384 BCE – 322 BCE, Aristotle, Constitution of the Athenians 16.8
      • 384 BCE – 322 BCE, Aristotle, Politics 1315B.21
      • 480 BCE – 411 BCE, Antiphon of Rhamnus, On the Murder of Herodes 13
      • 384 BCE – 322 BCE, Demosthenes, Against Timotheus 19
      • 423 BCE, Aristophanes, The Clouds 1277
      • 384 BCE – 322 BCE, Demosthenes, Against Macartatus 7
