Ancient Greek

[edit]

Alternative forms

[edit]
  • ἀθροΐζω (athroḯzō)
  • ἁθροίζω (hathroízō) — Old Attic

Etymology

[edit]

From ἀθρόος (athróos) +‎ -ίζω (-ízō).

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /a.tʰrǒi̯z.dɔː/ → /aˈθry.zo/ → /aˈθri.zo/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /a.tʰrǒi̯z.dɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /aˈtʰry.zo/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /aˈθry.zo/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /aˈθry.zo/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /aˈθri.zo/

Verb

[edit]

ᾰ̓θροίζω • (athroízō)

  1. to gather, to collect, to muster
