Ancient Greek

[edit]

Alternative forms

[edit]
  • ἐποράω (eporáō) — Ionic
  • ἐπόρημι (epórēmi) — Aeolic

Etymology

[edit]

ἐπι- (epi-) +‎ ὁράω (horáō)

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /e.pʰo.rá.ɔː/ → /e.ɸoˈra.o/ → /e.foˈra.o/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /e.pʰo.rá.ɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /e.pʰoˈra.o/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /e.ɸoˈra.o/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /e.foˈra.o/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /e.foˈra.o/

Verb

[edit]

ἐφορᾰ́ω • (ephoráō)

  1. to oversee, watch over
  2. to look upon, behold
