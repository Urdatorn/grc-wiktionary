Ancient Greek

[edit]

Alternative forms

[edit]
  • ξυνίστημι (xunístēmi) — Old Attic
  • συνιστάνω (sunistánō), συνιστάω (sunistáō)

Etymology

[edit]

συν- (sun-) +‎ ἵστημι (hístēmi)

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /sy.nís.tɛː.mi/ → /syˈnis.ti.mi/ → /siˈnis.ti.mi/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /sy.nís.tɛː.mi/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /syˈnis.te̝.mi/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /syˈnis.ti.mi/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /syˈnis.ti.mi/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /siˈnis.ti.mi/

Verb

[edit]

σῠνίστημῐ • (sunístēmi)

  1. to combine, unite
  2. to put together, organize
  3. to join, engage with (in a fight, in battle)
  4. to form a league or union, band together
  5. to place in charge of
