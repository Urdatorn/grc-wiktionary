Ancient Greek

[edit]

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /eː.lé.ɔː/ → /iˈle.o/ → /iˈle.o/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /eː.lé.ɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /iˈle.o/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /iˈle.o/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /iˈle.o/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /iˈle.o/

Verb

[edit]

εἰλέω • (eiléō)

  1. Alternative form of εἴλω (eílō, “roll up”)
