Ancient Greek

[edit]

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /a.lé.ɔː/ → /aˈle.o/ → /aˈle.o/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /a.lé.ɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /aˈle.o/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /aˈle.o/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /aˈle.o/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /aˈle.o/

Etymology 1

[edit]

From Proto-Indo-European *h₂elh₁- (“to grind”). Cognate with Old Armenian աղամ (ałam, “grind, pound”), Hindi आटा (āṭā, “flour”), Avestan 𐬀𐬴𐬀 (aṣ̌a, “ground”), Persian آرد (ârd, “flour”).

Verb

[edit]

ἀλέω • (aléō)

  1. (transitive) to grind, bruise, pound, mill
