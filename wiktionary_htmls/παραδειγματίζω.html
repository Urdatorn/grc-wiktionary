Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

From παρά (pará, “beside”) +‎ δειγματίζω (deigmatízō, “to show, demonstrate”).

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /pa.ra.deːŋ.ma.tíz.dɔː/ → /pa.ra.ðiɣ.maˈti.zo/ → /pa.ra.ðiɣ.maˈti.zo/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /pa.ra.deːŋ.ma.tíz.dɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /pa.ra.diɡ.maˈti.zo/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /pa.ra.ðiɣ.maˈti.zo/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /pa.ra.ðiɣ.maˈti.zo/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /pa.ra.ðiɣ.maˈti.zo/

Verb

[edit]

παραδειγματίζω • (paradeigmatízō)

  1. to make a public example of, shame, disgrace, expose
