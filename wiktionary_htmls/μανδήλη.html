Ancient Greek

[edit]

Alternative forms

[edit]
  • μαντήλα (mantḗla), μαντῆλα (mantêla)

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /manˈdi.li/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /manˈdi.li/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /manˈdi.li/

Noun

[edit]

μανδήλη • (mandḗlē) f (genitive μανδήλης); first declension

  1. (Byzantine) towel
