Ancient Greek

[edit]

Alternative forms

[edit]
  • μᾰ́λ’ (mál’) (apocopic form)

Etymology

[edit]

From Proto-Indo-European *mel- (“strong, better”). Cognates include Latin melius, multus and Latvian milns.[1]

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /má.la/ → /ˈma.la/ → /ˈma.la/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /má.la/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /ˈma.la/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /ˈma.la/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /ˈma.la/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /ˈma.la/
