Ancient Greek

[edit]

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /dý.nɔː/ → /ˈðy.no/ → /ˈði.no/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /dý.nɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /ˈdy.no/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /ˈðy.no/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /ˈðy.no/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /ˈði.no/

Verb

[edit]

δῠ́νω • (dúnō)

  1. Alternative form of δύω (dúō, “to plunge; to enter”)
