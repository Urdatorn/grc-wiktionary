Ancient Greek

[edit]

Alternative forms

[edit]
  • ἐνίσσω (eníssō)

Etymology

[edit]

From the same root as ἰάπτω (iáptō) and/or ἴπτομαι (íptomai).

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /e.níp.tɔː/ → /eˈnip.to/ → /eˈnip.to/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /e.níp.tɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /eˈnip.to/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /eˈnip.to/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /eˈnip.to/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /eˈnip.to/

Verb

[edit]

ἐνῑ́πτω • (enī́ptō)

  1. (chiefly Epic) to chide, rebuke, upbraid
