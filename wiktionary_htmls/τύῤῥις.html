Ancient Greek

[edit]

Alternative forms

[edit]
  • τύρσις (túrsis)

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /ˈtyr.ris/ → /ˈti.ris/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /ˈtyr.ris/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /ˈtyr.ris/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /ˈtyr.ris/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /ˈti.ris/

Noun

[edit]

τύῤῥις • (túrrhis) f (indeclinable)

  1. Alternative form of τύρσις (túrsis, “tower”)
    Synonyms: πύργος (púrgos), ἔπαλξις (épalxis), προμαχών (promakhṓn)

Usage notes

[edit]
  • Attested in Hesychius', who gives the definition as:
    • [5th c. C.E., Hesychius of Alexandria, Γλώσσαι, Τ:
      τύῤῥις· πύργος, ἔπαλξις, προμαχών
      túrrhis; púrgos, épalxis, promakhṓn
      túrrhis: tower, battlement, bastion]
