Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

From ἀνα- (ana-, intensifying affix) +‎ σπάω (spáō, “to draw”)

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /a.nas.pá.ɔː/ → /a.nasˈpa.o/ → /a.nasˈpa.o/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /a.nas.pá.ɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /a.nasˈpa.o/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /a.nasˈpa.o/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /a.nasˈpa.o/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /a.nasˈpa.o/

Verb

[edit]

ἀνασπάω • (anaspáō)

  1. to pull down
