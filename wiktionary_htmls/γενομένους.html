Ancient Greek

[edit]

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /ɡe.no.mé.nuːs/ → /ʝe.noˈme.nus/ → /ʝe.noˈme.nus/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /ɡe.no.mé.nuːs/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /ɡe.noˈme.nus/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /ʝe.noˈme.nus/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /ʝe.noˈme.nus/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /ʝe.noˈme.nus/

Participle

[edit]

γενομένους • (genoménous)

  1. masculine accusative plural of γενόμενος (genómenos)
