Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

From προ- (pro-, “before”) +‎ λῡ́ω (lū́ō, “loosen, dissolve”)

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /pro.ly̌ː.ɔː/ → /proˈly.o/ → /proˈli.o/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /pro.ly̌ː.ɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /proˈly.o/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /proˈly.o/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /proˈly.o/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /proˈli.o/

Verb

[edit]

προλῡ́ω • (prolū́ō)

  1. to undo, refute before
