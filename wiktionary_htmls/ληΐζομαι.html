Ancient Greek

[edit]

Alternative forms

[edit]
  • λεΐζομαι (leḯzomai)
  • λῄζομαι (lḗizomai) — contracted

Etymology

[edit]

From ληΐς (lēḯs, “booty”).

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /lɛː.íz.do.mai̯/ → /liˈi.zo.mɛ/ → /liˈi.zo.me/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /lɛː.íz.do.mai̯/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /le̝ˈi.zo.mɛ/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /liˈi.zo.mɛ/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /liˈi.zo.me/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /liˈi.zo.me/

Verb

[edit]

ληΐζομαι • (lēḯzomai)

  1. to despoil, plunder, carry off as booty
