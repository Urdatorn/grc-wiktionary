Ancient Greek

[edit]

Alternative forms

[edit]
  • ὑποργέω (huporgéō) — Ionic

Etymology

[edit]

From ὑπουργός (hupourgós, “rendering service, serviceable, conducive”) +‎ -έω (-éō).

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /hy.puːr.ɡé.ɔː/ → /y.purˈʝe.o/ → /i.purˈʝe.o/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /hy.puːr.ɡé.ɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /(h)y.purˈɡe.o/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /y.purˈʝe.o/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /y.purˈʝe.o/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /i.purˈʝe.o/

Verb

[edit]

ὑπουργέω • (hupourgéō)

  1. to render service or help to, serve, assist, succor [with dative]
