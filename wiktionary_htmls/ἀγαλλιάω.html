Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

Late form of ἀγάλλομαι (agállomai).

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /a.ɣal.liˈa.o/ → /a.ɣa.liˈa.o/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /a.ɡal.liˈa.o/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /a.ɣal.liˈa.o/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /a.ɣal.liˈa.o/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /a.ɣa.liˈa.o/

Verb

[edit]

ἀγαλλιάω • (agalliáō) (Koine)

  1. (intransitive) to be overjoyed, to rejoice

Usage notes

[edit]

Only used in Christian writings.
