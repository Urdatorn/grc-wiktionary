Ancient Greek

[edit]

Alternative forms

[edit]
  • ἐρᾱτύω (erātúō) — Doric

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /e.rɛː.tý.ɔː/ → /e.riˈty.o/ → /e.riˈti.o/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /e.rɛː.tý.ɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /e.re̝ˈty.o/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /e.riˈty.o/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /e.riˈty.o/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /e.riˈti.o/

Verb

[edit]

ἐρητύω • (erētúō) (Epic)

  1. (transitive) to keep back, restrain, check, bar
  2. to keep away from [with genitive]
