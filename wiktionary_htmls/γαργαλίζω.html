Ancient Greek

[edit]

Alternative forms

[edit]
  • γᾰγγᾰλῐζω (gangalizō)

Etymology

[edit]

Onomatopoeic formation with reduplication.

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /ɡar.ɡa.líz.dɔː/ → /ɣar.ɣaˈli.zo/ → /ɣar.ɣaˈli.zo/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /ɡar.ɡa.líz.dɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /ɡar.ɡaˈli.zo/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /ɣar.ɣaˈli.zo/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /ɣar.ɣaˈli.zo/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /ɣar.ɣaˈli.zo/

Verb

[edit]

γᾰργᾰλῐ́ζω • (gargalízō)

  1. (active voice) to tickle, titillate
    Synonym: κνῐ́ζω (knízō)
  2. (passive voice) to feel tickling or irritation
