Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

ἀνα- (ana-) +‎ ἵημι (híēmi)

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /a.ní.ɛː.mi/ → /aˈni.i.mi/ → /aˈni.i.mi/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /a.ní.ɛː.mi/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /aˈni.e̝.mi/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /aˈni.i.mi/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /aˈni.i.mi/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /aˈni.i.mi/

Verb

[edit]

ἀνίημῐ • (aníēmi)

  1. to let go, loosen
  2. to release
  3. to incite, impel, prompt
  4. to expose, lay bare
