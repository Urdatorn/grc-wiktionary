Ancient Greek

[edit]

Alternative forms

[edit]
  • κεραννύω (kerannúō)
  • κεραίω (keraíō), κεράω (keráō) — Epic
  • κίρνημι (kírnēmi) — Epic

Etymology

[edit]

From Proto-Hellenic *kírnāmi.

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /ke.rán.nyː.mi/ → /ceˈran.ny.mi/ → /ceˈra.ni.mi/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /ke.rán.nyː.mi/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /keˈran.ny.mi/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /ceˈran.ny.mi/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /ceˈran.ny.mi/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /ceˈra.ni.mi/

Verb

[edit]

κεράννῡμῐ • (keránnūmi)

  1. to mix, mingle, blend
  2. to temper, cool by mixing
  3. to compound
  4. (grammar, passive voice) to coalesce by crasis
  5. to multiply into
