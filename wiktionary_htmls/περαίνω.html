Ancient Greek

[edit]

Alternative forms

[edit]
  • πειραίνω (peiraínō) — Epic, Ionic

Etymology

[edit]

πεῖραρ (peîrar, “end”) +‎ -ι̯ω (-i̯ō).

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /pe.rǎi̯.nɔː/ → /peˈrɛ.no/ → /peˈre.no/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /pe.rǎi̯.nɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /peˈrɛ.no/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /peˈrɛ.no/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /peˈre.no/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /peˈre.no/

Verb

[edit]

περαίνω • (peraínō)

  1. to finish, to bring to an end
  2. to limit
  3. to effect
  4. to reach
  5. to come to an end
  6. to penetrate
