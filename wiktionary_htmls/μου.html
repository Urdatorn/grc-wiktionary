Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

From Ancient Greek μου (mou).

Pronoun

[edit]

μου • (mou) (possessive and genitive)

  1. (personal, indirect object) me
    Μου είπε την αλήθεια.
    Mou eípe tin alítheia.
    He told me the truth.
  2. (possessive) my
    Το παιδί μου κι εγώ.
    To paidí mou ki egó.
    My child and I.
    το όνομά μου είναι …
    to ónomá mou eínai …
    my name is …

Synonyms

[edit]
  • (personal): εμένα (eména) (strong form)

Interjection

[edit]

μου • (mou)

  1. (onomatopoeia) moo; the characteristic sound made by a cow or bull.
