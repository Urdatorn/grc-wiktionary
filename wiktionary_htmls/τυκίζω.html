Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

From τῠ́κος (túkos, “an instrument for stonemasons”) +‎ -ίζω (-ízō).

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /ty.kíz.dɔː/ → /tyˈci.zo/ → /tiˈci.zo/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /ty.kíz.dɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /tyˈki.zo/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /tyˈci.zo/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /tyˈci.zo/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /tiˈci.zo/

Verb

[edit]

τῠκῐ́ζω • (tukízō)

  1. to work stones
