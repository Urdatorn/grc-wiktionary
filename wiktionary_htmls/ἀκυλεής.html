Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

Likely borrowed from Latin aquila.

Noun

[edit]

ἀκυλεής • (akuleḗs)

  1. eagle
    Synonym: ἀετός (aetós)
    • [5th c. C.E., Hesychius of Alexandria, Γλώσσαι, Α.2687:
      ἀκυλεής· ἀετός
      akuleḗs; aetós
      akuleḗs: eagle]
