Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

From ἐπί (epí, “on”) + εἰ (ei, “if”).

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /e.pěː/ → /eˈpi/ → /eˈpi/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /e.pěː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /eˈpi/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /eˈpi/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /eˈpi/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /eˈpi/
