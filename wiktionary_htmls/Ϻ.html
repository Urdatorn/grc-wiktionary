Etymology

[edit]

This letter is derived from the Phoenician letter 𐤑 (ṣ, “tsade”)

Letter

[edit]

Ϻ • (Ś) (upper case, lower case ϻ)

  1. (historical) The letter san, which was used for the sound /s/ but eventually abandoned in favor of Σ.
