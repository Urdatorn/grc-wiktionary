Etymology

[edit]
    Etymology tree
    Proto-Indo-European *pnew-
    Ancient Greek πνέω (pnéō)

    From Proto-Indo-European *pnew- (“to breathe, sneeze”). Cognates include Old English fnēosan (English sneeze).[1]
