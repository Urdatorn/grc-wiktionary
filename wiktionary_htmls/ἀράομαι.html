Ancient Greek

[edit]

Alternative forms

[edit]
  • ἄραμαι (áramai) — Lesbian
  • ἀρῶμαι (arômai) — Attic

Etymology

[edit]

From ἀρᾱ́ (arā́, “prayer”) +‎ -ᾰ́ω (-áō).

Verb

[edit]

ᾰ̓ράομαι or ᾱ̓ράομαι • (aráomai or āráomai)

  1. (middle only) to pray
