Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

κᾰτᾰ- (kata-) +‎ νεύω (neúō)

Verb

[edit]

κᾰτᾰνεύω • (kataneúō)

  1. to nod
  2. to beckon
  3. to grant, promise
