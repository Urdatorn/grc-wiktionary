Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

προ- (pro-) +‎ χωρέω (khōréō)

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /pro.kʰɔː.ré.ɔː/ → /pro.xoˈre.o/ → /pro.xoˈre.o/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /pro.kʰɔː.ré.ɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /pro.kʰoˈre.o/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /pro.xoˈre.o/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /pro.xoˈre.o/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /pro.xoˈre.o/

Verb

[edit]

προχωρέω • (prokhōréō)

  1. to advance, proceed, progress
  2. to prosper, succeed
