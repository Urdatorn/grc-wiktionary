Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

ἀπο- (apo-) +‎ λαμβάνω (lambánō)

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /a.po.lam.bá.nɔː/ → /a.po.lamˈba.no/ → /a.po.lamˈba.no/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /a.po.lam.bá.nɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /a.po.lamˈba.no/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /a.po.lamˈba.no/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /a.po.lamˈba.no/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /a.po.lamˈba.no/

Verb

[edit]

ἀπολαμβάνω • (apolambánō)

  1. to receive
  2. to take back, recover
  3. to take aside
