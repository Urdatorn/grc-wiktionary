Ancient Greek

[edit]

Alternative forms

[edit]
  • ξυσκοτάζω (xuskotázō) — Old Attic

Etymology

[edit]

From συν- (sun-) +‎ σκότος (skótos, “darkness”) +‎ -άζω (-ázō). The unprefixed verb σκοτάζω (skotázō) is attested later.

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /sys.ko.táz.dɔː/ → /sys.koˈta.zo/ → /sis.koˈta.zo/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /sys.ko.táz.dɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /sys.koˈta.zo/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /sys.koˈta.zo/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /sys.koˈta.zo/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /sis.koˈta.zo/

Verb

[edit]

συσκοτάζω • (suskotázō)

  1. (intransitive) to grow quite dark
  2. (Koine, transitive) to make dark, darken
