Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

ἀπο- (apo-) +‎ βαίνω (baínō)

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /a.po.bǎi̯.nɔː/ → /a.poˈβɛ.no/ → /a.poˈve.no/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /a.po.bǎi̯.nɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /a.poˈbɛ.no/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /a.poˈβɛ.no/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /a.poˈve.no/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /a.poˈve.no/

Verb

[edit]

ἀποβαίνω • (apobaínō)

  1. to go away, depart, leave
  2. to alight (from a chariot)
  3. to disembark (from a ship)
