Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /sûː/ → /su/ → /su/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /sûː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /su/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /su/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /su/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /su/

Pronoun

[edit]

σοῦ • (soû)

  1. stressed genitive singular of σύ (sú)

Alternative forms

[edit]
  • σου (sou) — unstressed

Adjective

[edit]

σοῦ • (soû)

  1. genitive singular masculine/neuter of σός (sós)
