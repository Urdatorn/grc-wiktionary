Ancient Greek

[edit]

Alternative forms

[edit]
  • παιωνίζω (paiōnízō)

Etymology

[edit]

From παιᾱ́ν (paiā́n) +‎ -ίζω (-ízō)

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /pai̯.aː.níz.dɔː/ → /pɛ.aˈni.zo/ → /pe.aˈni.zo/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /pai̯.aː.níz.dɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /pɛ.aˈni.zo/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /pɛ.aˈni.zo/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /pe.aˈni.zo/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /pe.aˈni.zo/

Verb

[edit]

παιᾱνίζω • (paiānízō)

  1. to sing a paean, sing in triumph
