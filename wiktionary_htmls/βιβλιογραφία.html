Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

From βιβλίον (biblíon) +‎ -γραφίᾱ (-graphíā) and/or βιβλιογράφος (bibliográphos) +‎ -ίᾱ (-íā).

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /βi.βli.o.ɣraˈɸi.a/ → /vi.vli.o.ɣraˈfi.a/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /bi.bli.o.ɡraˈpʰi.a/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /βi.βli.o.ɣraˈɸi.a/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /vi.vli.o.ɣraˈfi.a/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /vi.vli.o.ɣraˈfi.a/

Noun

[edit]

βιβλῐογρᾰφίᾱ • (bibliographíā) f (genitive βιβλῐογρᾰφίᾱς); first declension

  1. (Koine) writing of books
