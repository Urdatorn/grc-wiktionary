Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

Intensive reduplicated formation with unclear -ρ-, probably connected to δᾰ́πτω (dáptō, “to devour, consume”).

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /dar.dáp.tɔː/ → /ðarˈðap.to/ → /ðarˈðap.to/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /dar.dáp.tɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /darˈdap.to/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /ðarˈðap.to/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /ðarˈðap.to/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /ðarˈðap.to/

Verb

[edit]

δᾰρδᾰ́πτω • (dardáptō)

  1. to eat, devour, as wild beasts
    Synonym: δᾰ́πτω (dáptō)
