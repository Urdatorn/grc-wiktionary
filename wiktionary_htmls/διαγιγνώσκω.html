Ancient Greek

[edit]

Alternative forms

[edit]
  • διαγῑνώσκω (diagīnṓskō) — Ionic, Koine

Etymology

[edit]

From δια- (dia-) +‎ γιγνώσκω (gignṓskō).

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /di.a.ɡi.ɡnɔ̌ːs.kɔː/ → /ði.a.ʝiˈɣnos.ko/ → /ði.a.ʝiˈɣnos.ko/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /di.a.ɡi.ɡnɔ̌ːs.kɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /di.a.ɡiˈɡnos.ko/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /ði.a.ʝiˈɣnos.ko/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /ði.a.ʝiˈɣnos.ko/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /ði.a.ʝiˈɣnos.ko/

Verb

[edit]

διαγιγνώσκω • (diagignṓskō)

  1. to distinguish, discern
  2. to resolve, vote to do something
  3. (law) to decide (a suit), give judgment
