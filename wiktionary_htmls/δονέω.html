  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /do.né.ɔː/

  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /ðoˈne.o/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /ðoˈne.o/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /ðoˈne.o/

Verb

[edit]

δονέω • (donéō)

  1. to shake, cause to quiver, especially of the wind
  2. to drive about; disturb, terrify
  3. (of love) to agitate, excite
  4. (passive voice, of troops) to wheel
  5. (of sound) to murmur, buzz, rouse
