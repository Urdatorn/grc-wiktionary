Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

From βρᾰδῠ́ς (bradús, “slow”).

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /bra.dý.nɔː/ → /βraˈðy.no/ → /vraˈði.no/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /bra.dý.nɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /braˈdy.no/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /βraˈðy.no/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /vraˈðy.no/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /vraˈði.no/

Verb

[edit]

βρᾰδῠ́νω • (bradúnō)

  1. to make slow, delay
  2. (intransitive) to loiter, delay
