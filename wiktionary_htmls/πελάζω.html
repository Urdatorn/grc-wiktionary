Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

From πέλας (pélas, “nearby”) +‎ -άζω (-ázō). Compare cognate πλησιάζω (plēsiázō) and old πιλνάω (pilnáō).

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /pe.láz.dɔː/ → /peˈla.zo/ → /peˈla.zo/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /pe.láz.dɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /peˈla.zo/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /peˈla.zo/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /peˈla.zo/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /peˈla.zo/

Verb

[edit]

πελάζω • (pelázō)

  1. (intransitive) to approach
  2. (transitive) to bring near
