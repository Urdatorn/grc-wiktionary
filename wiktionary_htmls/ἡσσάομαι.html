Ancient Greek

[edit]

Alternative forms

[edit]
  • ἡττάομαι (hēttáomai) — Attic
  • ἑσσάομαι (hessáomai) — Ionic

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /hɛːs.sá.o.mai̯/ → /isˈsa.o.mɛ/ → /iˈsa.o.me/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /hɛːs.sá.o.mai̯/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /(h)e̝sˈsa.o.mɛ/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /isˈsa.o.mɛ/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /isˈsa.o.me/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /iˈsa.o.me/

Verb

[edit]

ἡσσᾰ́ομαι • (hēssáomai)

  1. to be less or weaker than, inferior to
    1. (passive voice) to be defeated, discomfited
    2. (law) to be cast in a suit
    3. (genitive) to give way, yield
    4. (dative) to be overcome by
  2. (late, active voice, deponent) to overcome, defeat
