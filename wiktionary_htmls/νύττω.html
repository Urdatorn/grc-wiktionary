Ancient Greek

[edit]

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /nýt.tɔː/ → /ˈnyt.to/ → /ˈni.to/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /nýt.tɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /ˈnyt.to/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /ˈnyt.to/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /ˈnyt.to/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /ˈni.to/

Verb

[edit]

νῠ́ττω • (núttō)

  1. Attic form of νύσσω (nússō)
