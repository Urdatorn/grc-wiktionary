Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

From ὑπερ- (huper-, “beyond”) +‎ νῑκᾰ́ω (nīkáō, “win, conquer”), literally “win beyond”.

Verb

[edit]

ὑπερνικάω • (hupernikáō)

  1. to win or conquer beyond, to be beyond a winner or a conqueror,
