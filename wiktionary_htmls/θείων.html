Ancient Greek

[edit]

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /tʰěː.ɔːn/ → /ˈθi.on/ → /ˈθi.on/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /tʰěː.ɔːn/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /ˈtʰi.on/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /ˈθi.on/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /ˈθi.on/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /ˈθi.on/

Participle

[edit]

θείων • (theíōn) m (feminine θείουσᾰ, neuter θεῖον); first/third declension

  1. present active participle of θείω (theíō)

Adjective

[edit]

θείων • (theíōn)

  1. masculine/feminine/neuter genitive plural of θεῖος (theîos)

Noun

[edit]

θείων • (theíōn)

  1. genitive plural of θεῖος (theîos)
  2. genitive plural of θεῖον (theîon)

Greek

[edit]

Adjective

[edit]

θείων • (theíon)

  1. Genitive masculine, feminine and neuter plural form of θείος (theíos).

Noun

[edit]

θείων • (theíon)

  1. (masculine): Genitive plural form of θείος (theíos).
  2. (feminine): Genitive plural form of θεία (theía).
