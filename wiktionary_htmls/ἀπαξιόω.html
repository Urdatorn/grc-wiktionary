Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

ἀπο- (apo-) +‎ ἀξιόω (axióō)

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /a.pak.si.ó.ɔː/ → /a.pak.siˈo.o/ → /a.pak.siˈo.o/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /a.pak.si.ó.ɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /a.pak.siˈo.o/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /a.pak.siˈo.o/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /a.pak.siˈo.o/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /a.pak.siˈo.o/

Verb

[edit]

ἀπαξῐόω • (apaxióō)

  1. to deem unworthy, disown, banish
