Ancient Greek

[edit]

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /drɔ̌ːp.tɔː/ → /ˈðrop.to/ → /ˈðrop.to/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /drɔ̌ːp.tɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /ˈdrop.to/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /ˈðrop.to/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /ˈðrop.to/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /ˈðrop.to/

Verb

[edit]

δρώπτω • (drṓptō)

  1. to look
