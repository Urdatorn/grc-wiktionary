Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

ἄπιστος (ápistos) +‎ -έω (-éō)

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /a.pis.té.ɔː/ → /a.pisˈte.o/ → /a.pisˈte.o/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /a.pis.té.ɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /a.pisˈte.o/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /a.pisˈte.o/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /a.pisˈte.o/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /a.pisˈte.o/

Verb

[edit]

ἀπιστέω • (apistéō)

  1. to disbelieve, doubt
  2. to distrust, mistrust
  3. (Koine) to betray a trust, be unfaithful
