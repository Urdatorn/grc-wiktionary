Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

From ἐγ- (eg-) +‎ γράφω (gráphō)

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /eŋ.ɡrá.pʰɔː/ → /eŋˈɡra.ɸo/ → /eŋˈɡra.fo/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /eŋ.ɡrá.pʰɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /eŋˈɡra.pʰo/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /eŋˈɡra.ɸo/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /eŋˈɡra.fo/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /eŋˈɡra.fo/

Verb

[edit]

ἐγγράφω • (engráphō)

  1. to mark in or on, to paint on
  2. to inscribe, write in or on
  3. to enter on a list or register
  4. to indict
