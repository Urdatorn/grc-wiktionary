Verb

ψεύδομαι • (pseúdomai)

  1. first-person singular present mediopassive indicative of ψεύδω (pseúdō)

Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

Learnedly, from Ancient Greek ψεύδομαι (pseúdomai), from ψεύδω (pseúdō).

Pronunciation

[edit]
  • IPA(key): /ˈpse.vðo.me/
  • Hyphenation: ψεύ‧δο‧μαι

Verb

[edit]

ψεύδομαι • (psévdomai) deponent found only in the present and imperfect tenses [1] & rare past tense ψεύσθηκα (pséfsthika) [2]

  1. (formal) to lie, tell lies
    common: λέω ψέματα (léo psémata)
