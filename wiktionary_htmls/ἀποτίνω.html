Verb

[edit]

ἀποτῐ́νω • (apotínō) (Attic)
ἀποτῑ́νω • (apotī́nō) (Epic)

  1. to pay back, repay, return
  2. to pay for
  3. to make atonement
  4. to pay in full
  5. (middle voice) to exact or require a penalty from
  6. to avenge oneself on, punish
  7. to take vengeance for, punish (a misdeed etc.)
  8. to take vengeance
