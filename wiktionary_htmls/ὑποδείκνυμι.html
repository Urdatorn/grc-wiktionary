Ancient Greek

[edit]

Alternative forms

[edit]
  • ὑποδεικνύω (hupodeiknúō)

Etymology

[edit]

ῠ̔πο- (hupo-) +‎ δείκνῡμῐ (deíknūmi)

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /hy.po.děː.knyː.mi/ → /y.poˈði.kny.mi/ → /i.poˈði.kni.mi/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /hy.po.děː.knyː.mi/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /(h)y.poˈdi.kny.mi/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /y.poˈði.kny.mi/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /y.poˈði.kny.mi/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /i.poˈði.kni.mi/

Verb

[edit]

ῠ̔ποδείκνῡμῐ • (hupodeíknūmi)

  1. to show
  2. to intimate
  3. to teach
  4. to pretend
