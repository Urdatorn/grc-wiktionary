Ancient Greek

[edit]

Alternative forms

[edit]
  • κατάπτω (katáptō) — Ionic

Etymology

[edit]

κατα- (kata-) +‎ ἅπτω (háptō)

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /ka.tʰáp.tɔː/ → /kaˈθap.to/ → /kaˈθap.to/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /ka.tʰáp.tɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /kaˈtʰap.to/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /kaˈθap.to/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /kaˈθap.to/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /kaˈθap.to/

Verb

[edit]

καθάπτω • (katháptō)

  1. to fasten, make fast
  2. (Epic, in middle) to address, accost
