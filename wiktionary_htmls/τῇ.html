Ancient Greek

[edit]

Alternative forms

[edit]
  • τᾷ (tâi) — Doric, Aeolic

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /tɛ̂ːi̯/ → /ti/ → /ti/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /tɛ̂ːi̯/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /te̝/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /ti/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /ti/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /ti/

Article

[edit]

τῇ • (têi)

  1. (Attic, Epic, Ionic) feminine dative singular of ὁ (ho)

Adverb

[edit]
  1. Here, there.
