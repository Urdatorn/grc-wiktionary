Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

κατα- (kata-) +‎ ἄγω (ágō)

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /ka.tá.ɡɔː/ → /kaˈta.ɣo/ → /kaˈta.ɣo/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /ka.tá.ɡɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /kaˈta.ɡo/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /kaˈta.ɣo/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /kaˈta.ɣo/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /kaˈta.ɣo/

Verb

[edit]

κᾰτάγω • (katágō)

  1. to lead down, bring down, draw
  2. to reduce
  3. to escort
  4. to derive
  5. to lower
  6. to bring back, bring home, recall, restore
  7. (passive voice) to return
  8. (Byzantine) to tear down
