Ancient Greek

[edit]

Alternative forms

[edit]
  • κυκανάω (kukanáō) — Poetic

Etymology

[edit]

Uncertain. Compare Sanskrit खजति (khajati)

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /ky.ká.ɔː/ → /cyˈka.o/ → /ciˈka.o/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /ky.ká.ɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /kyˈka.o/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /cyˈka.o/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /cyˈka.o/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /ciˈka.o/

Verb

[edit]

κῠκᾰ́ω • (kukáō)

  1. to stir up, mix
  2. to stir up, throw into confusion, confound
