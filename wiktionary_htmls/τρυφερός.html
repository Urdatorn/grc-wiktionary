Ancient Greek

[edit]

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /try.pʰe.rós/ → /try.ɸeˈros/ → /tri.feˈros/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /try.pʰe.rós/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /try.pʰeˈros/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /try.ɸeˈros/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /try.feˈros/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /tri.feˈros/

Adjective

[edit]

τρῠφερός • (trupherós) m (feminine τρῠφερᾱ́, neuter τρῠφερόν); first/second declension

  1. delicate, dainty
