Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

Personification of φόβος (phóbos).

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /pʰó.bos/ → /ˈɸo.βos/ → /ˈfo.vos/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /pʰó.bos/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /ˈpʰo.bos/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /ˈɸo.βos/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /ˈfo.vos/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /ˈfo.vos/

Proper noun

[edit]

Φόβος • (Phóbos) m (genitive Φόβου); second declension

  1. (Greek mythology) Phobos (the Greek god of fear)

Proper noun

[edit]

Φόβος • (Fóvos) m

  1. (Greek mythology) Phobos (Greek god of fear)
