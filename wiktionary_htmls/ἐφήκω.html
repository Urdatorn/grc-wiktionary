Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

From ἐπι- (epi-) +‎ ἥκω (hḗkō)

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /e.pʰɛ̌ː.kɔː/ → /eˈɸi.ko/ → /eˈfi.ko/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /e.pʰɛ̌ː.kɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /eˈpʰe̝.ko/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /eˈɸi.ko/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /eˈfi.ko/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /eˈfi.ko/

Verb

[edit]

ἐφήκω • (ephḗkō)

  1. to have arrived
  2. to reach, extend (a certain distance)
  3. to occupy (a space)
