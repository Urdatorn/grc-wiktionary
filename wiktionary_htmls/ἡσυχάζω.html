Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

ἥσυχος (hḗsukhos, “quiet”) +‎ -ᾰ́ζω (-ázō)

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /hɛː.sy.kʰáz.dɔː/ → /i.syˈxa.zo/ → /i.siˈxa.zo/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /hɛː.sy.kʰáz.dɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /(h)e̝.syˈkʰa.zo/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /i.syˈxa.zo/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /i.syˈxa.zo/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /i.siˈxa.zo/

Verb

[edit]

ἡσῠχᾰ́ζω • (hēsukházō)

  1. to be still or quiet, be at rest
