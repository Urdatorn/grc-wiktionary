Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

μηχανή (mēkhanḗ) +‎ -άομαι (-áomai)

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /mɛː.kʰa.ná.o.mai̯/ → /mi.xaˈna.o.mɛ/ → /mi.xaˈna.o.me/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /mɛː.kʰa.ná.o.mai̯/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /me̝.kʰaˈna.o.mɛ/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /mi.xaˈna.o.mɛ/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /mi.xaˈna.o.me/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /mi.xaˈna.o.me/

Verb

[edit]

μηχᾰνάομαι • (mēkhanáomai)

  1. to build, construct
  2. to devise, contrive
