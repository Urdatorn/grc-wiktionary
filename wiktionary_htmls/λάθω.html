Ancient Greek

[edit]

Alternative forms

[edit]
  • λήθω (lḗthō) — Ionic

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /lǎː.tʰɔː/ → /ˈla.θo/ → /ˈla.θo/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /lǎː.tʰɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /ˈla.tʰo/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /ˈla.θo/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /ˈla.θo/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /ˈla.θo/

Verb

[edit]

λᾱ́θω • (lā́thō) (Doric)

  1. Alternative form of λανθάνω (lanthánō, “to escape notice”)
