Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

From ἀντί (antí) +‎ -άω (-áō). See also ἀντιάω (antiáō).

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /an.tá.ɔː/ → /anˈta.o/ → /anˈda.o/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /an.tá.ɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /anˈta.o/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /anˈta.o/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /anˈta.o/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /anˈda.o/

Verb

[edit]

ἀντάω • (antáō)

  1. to come opposite to, meet face to face, meet with
