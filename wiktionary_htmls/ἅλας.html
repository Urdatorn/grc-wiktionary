Ancient Greek

[edit]

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /ˈa.las/ → /ˈa.las/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /ˈ(h)a.las/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /ˈa.las/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /ˈa.las/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /ˈa.las/

Noun

[edit]

ἅλας • (hálas) n (genitive ἅλατος); third declension

  1. Koine form of ἅλς (háls, “salt”)

Declension

[edit]
Third declension of τὸ ἅλας; τοῦ ἅλατος (Koine)
Case / # Singular
Nominative τὸ ἅλας
tò hálas
Genitive τοῦ ἅλατος
toû hálatos
Dative τῷ ἅλατῐ
tôi hálati
Accusative τὸ ἅλας
tò hálas
Vocative ἅλας
hálas
Notes:
  • Nominative singular -ς (-s) arose by reduction of the original cluster *-ts.

Descendants

[edit]
  • Greek: αλάτι (aláti)
  • → Greek: άλας (álas)
