Ancient Greek

[edit]

Alternative forms

[edit]
  • τόκα (tóka) — Doric
  • τόθ’ (tóth’) — apocopic, before a rough breathing
  • τότ’ (tót’) — apocopic, before a smooth breathing

Etymology

[edit]

Proto-Hellenic *tókʷe, from Proto-Indo-European *to- + *-kʷe. Compare ὅτε (hóte), πότε (póte). Cognate with Latin tum.[1]

See its indefinite form τοτέ (toté).
