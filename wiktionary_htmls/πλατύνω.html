Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

From πλᾰτῠ́ς (platús, “wide, broad”) +‎ -νω (-nō, verbal suffix).

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /pla.tý.nɔː/ → /plaˈty.no/ → /plaˈti.no/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /pla.tý.nɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /plaˈty.no/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /plaˈty.no/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /plaˈty.no/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /plaˈti.no/

Verb

[edit]

πλᾰτῠ́νω • (platúnō)

  1. to widen
  2. to pronounce broadly
  3. to amplify
