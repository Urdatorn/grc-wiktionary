Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

κατα- (kata-) +‎ πῑ́πτω (pī́ptō)

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /ka.ta.pǐːp.tɔː/ → /ka.taˈpip.to/ → /ka.taˈpip.to/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /ka.ta.pǐːp.tɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /ka.taˈpip.to/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /ka.taˈpip.to/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /ka.taˈpip.to/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /ka.taˈpip.to/

Verb

[edit]

κᾰτᾰπῑ́πτω • (katapī́ptō)

  1. to fall down
