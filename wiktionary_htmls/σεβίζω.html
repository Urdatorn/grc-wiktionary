Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

From σέβας (sébas, “awe”) +‎ -ίζω (-ízō, verb suffix).

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /se.bíz.dɔː/ → /seˈβi.zo/ → /seˈvi.zo/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /se.bíz.dɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /seˈbi.zo/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /seˈβi.zo/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /seˈvi.zo/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /seˈvi.zo/

Verb

[edit]

σεβῐ́ζω • (sebízō)

  1. to worship, honour
