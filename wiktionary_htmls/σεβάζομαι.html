Ancient Greek

[edit]

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /se.báz.do.mai̯/ → /seˈβa.zo.mɛ/ → /seˈva.zo.me/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /se.báz.do.mai̯/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /seˈba.zo.mɛ/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /seˈβa.zo.mɛ/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /seˈva.zo.me/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /seˈva.zo.me/

Verb

[edit]

σεβάζομαι • (sebázomai)

  1. to fear, be afraid
  2. to honour religiously, to worship
  3. (post-Homeric) Synonym of σέβομαι (sébomai)
