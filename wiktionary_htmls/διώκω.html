Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

Learned borrowing from Ancient Greek διώκω (diṓkō).

Pronunciation

[edit]
  • IPA(key): /ðiˈo.ko/
  • Hyphenation: δι‧ώ‧κω

Verb

[edit]

διώκω • (dióko) (past δίωξα, passive διώκομαι)

  1. to persecute
  2. to seek
  3. (law) to prosecute
