Etymology

[edit]

From μυριάς (muriás, “number of 10,000”), from μυρίος (muríos, “numberless, countless, infinite”).

Numeral

[edit]

μύριοι • (múrioi) (feminine μύριες, neuter μύρια)

  1. ten thousand, myriad
