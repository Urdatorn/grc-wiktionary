Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

From the root of σκεδάννῡμι (skedánnūmi).

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /skí.dnɛː.mi/ → /ˈsci.ðni.mi/ → /ˈsci.ðni.mi/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /skí.dnɛː.mi/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /ˈski.dne̝.mi/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /ˈsci.ðni.mi/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /ˈsci.ðni.mi/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /ˈsci.ðni.mi/

Verb

[edit]

σκίδνημι • (skídnēmi)

  1. to scatter, disperse
