Ancient Greek

[edit]

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /he.ɔː.y.tûː/ → /e.o.yˈtu/ → /e.o.iˈtu/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /he.ɔː.y.tûː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /(h)e.o.yˈtu/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /e.o.yˈtu/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /e.o.yˈtu/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /e.o.iˈtu/

Pronoun

[edit]

ἑωυτοῦ • (heōutoû)

  1. Ionic form of ἑαυτοῦ (heautoû)
