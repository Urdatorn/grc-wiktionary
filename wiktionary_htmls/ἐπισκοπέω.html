Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

From ἐπι- (epi-) +‎ σκοπέω (skopéō).

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /e.pis.ko.pé.ɔː/ → /e.pis.koˈpe.o/ → /e.pis.koˈpe.o/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /e.pis.ko.pé.ɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /e.pis.koˈpe.o/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /e.pis.koˈpe.o/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /e.pis.koˈpe.o/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /e.pis.koˈpe.o/

Verb

[edit]

ἐπισκοπέω • (episkopéō)

  1. to inspect, observe, examine, regard
  2. to watch over
  3. to visit
  4. to inspect, review (of an army general)
  5. to consider, reflect
  6. (middle voice) to meditate
