Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

From Phoenician 𐤆 (z, “zayin”).

Letter

[edit]

Ζ • (Z) (uppercase, lowercase ζ)

  1. The letter zeta (ζῆτα), the sixth letter of the ancient Greek alphabet.

Derived terms

[edit]
  • (Cyrillic) З
  • (Coptic) Ⲍ
  • (Gothic) 𐌶

Greek

[edit]

Letter

[edit]

Ζ • (Z) (uppercase, lowercase ζ)

  1. The upper case letter zeta (ζήτα), the sixth letter of the modern Greek alphabet.
