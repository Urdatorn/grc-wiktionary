Etymology 1

[edit]

From θεῖον (theîon, “sulphur”) +‎ -όω (-óō).

Verb

[edit]

θειόω • (theióō)

  1. to smoke with brimstone, to fumigate and purify
    1. to purify, hallow

Etymology 2

[edit]

From θεῖος (theîos, “divine”) +‎ -όω (-óō).

Verb

[edit]

θειόω • (theióō)

  1. to make divine, dedicate to a god
