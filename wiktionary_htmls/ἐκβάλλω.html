Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

From ἐκ- (ek-, “out”) +‎ βάλλω (bállō, “to throw”).

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /eɡ.bál.lɔː/ → /eɡˈβal.lo/ → /eɡˈva.lo/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /eɡ.bál.lɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /eɡˈbal.lo/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /eɡˈβal.lo/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /eɡˈval.lo/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /eɡˈva.lo/
