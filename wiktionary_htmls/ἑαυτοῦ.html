Ancient Greek

[edit]

Alternative forms

[edit]
  • ἑωυτοῦ (heōutoû) — Ionic
  • αὑτοῦ (hautoû) — Attic, contracted
  • αὐταύτου (autaútou) — Doric

Etymology

[edit]

Contraction of ἕο (héo, “him”) +‎ αὐτοῦ (autoû, “self”).

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /he.au̯.tûː/ → /e.aɸˈtu/ → /e.afˈtu/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /he.au̯.tûː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /(h)e.aʍˈtu/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /e.aɸˈtu/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /e.afˈtu/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /e.afˈtu/

Pronoun

[edit]

ἑαυτοῦ • (heautoû) (reflexive, reciprocal, Attic)

  1. (3rd person) himself, herself, itself, themselves
    • 620 BCE – 500 BCE, Alcaeus of Mytilene, Collected Works 78
    • 366 BCE – 348 BCE, Plato, Theaetetus 152B
    • 460 BCE – 395 BCE, Thucydides, History of the Peloponnesian War 1.141
    • 366 BCE – 348 BCE, Plato, Theaetetus 157A
    • 460 BCE – 395 BCE, Thucydides, History of the Peloponnesian War 5.60
    • 430 BCE – 354 BCE, Xenophon, Memorabilia 2.10.3
    • 430 BCE – 354 BCE, Xenophon, Memorabilia 3.13.3
    • 460 BCE – 420 BCE, Herodotus, Histories 8.86
    • 460 BCE – 395 BCE, Thucydides, History of the Peloponnesian War 1.8
    • 428 BCE – 347 BCE, Plato, Protagoras 350A
    • 460 BCE – 420 BCE, Herodotus, Histories 2.8
    • 460 BCE – 420 BCE, Herodotus, Histories 4.85
  2. (Attic and Koine) used for first or second person: myself, yourself
    • 458 BCE, Aeschylus, The Libation Bearers 221
    • 429 BCE, Sophocles, Oedipus the King 138
    • 458 BCE, Aeschylus, Agamemnon 1297
    • 428 BCE – 347 BCE, Plato, Phaedo 101C
    • 280 BCE – 220 BCE, Philo of Byzantium, Belopoeica 59.16
    • 460 BCE – 395 BCE, Thucydides, History of the Peloponnesian War 1.82
    • 341 BCE – 270 BCE, Epicurus, Vatican Sayings 47
    • 300 BCE – 200 BCE, 1 Samuel 14.9
    • 55 CE – 56 CE, Paul the Apostle, Second Epistle to the Corinthians 7:1
  3. (in the plural) of one another
    • 460 BCE – 420 BCE, Herodotus, Histories 3.49
    • 460 BCE – 395 BCE, Thucydides, History of the Peloponnesian War 4.25
    • 442 BCE, Sophocles, Antigone 144
    • 330 BCE, Demosthenes, On the Crown 19
    • 384 BCE – 322 BCE, Demosthenes, First Philippic 10
    • 400 BCE – 387 BCE, Plato, Lysis 215B

Usage notes

[edit]

Dual forms of reflexive pronouns are rarely, if at all, attested.
