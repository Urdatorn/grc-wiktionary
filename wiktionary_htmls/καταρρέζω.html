Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

From κατα- (kata-) +‎ ῥέζω (rhézō).

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /ka.tar̥.r̥éz.dɔː/ → /ka.tarˈre.zo/ → /ka.taˈre.zo/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /ka.tar̥.r̥éz.dɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /ka.tarˈre.zo/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /ka.tarˈre.zo/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /ka.tarˈre.zo/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /ka.taˈre.zo/

Verb

[edit]

κᾰταρρέζω • (katarrhézō)

  1. to pat, stroke, caress
