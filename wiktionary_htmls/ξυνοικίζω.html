Ancient Greek

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /ksy.noi̯.kíz.dɔː/ → /ksy.nyˈci.zo/ → /ksi.niˈci.zo/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /ksy.noi̯.kíz.dɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /ksy.nyˈki.zo/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /ksy.nyˈci.zo/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /ksy.nyˈci.zo/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /ksi.niˈci.zo/

Verb

[edit]

ξῠνοικίζω • (xunoikízō)

  1. Old Attic form of συνοικίζω (sunoikízō)
