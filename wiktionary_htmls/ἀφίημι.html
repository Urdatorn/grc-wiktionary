Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

From ἀφ- (aph-, “from, away from”) + ἵημι (híēmi, “to send, throw”).

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /a.pʰǐː.ɛː.mi/ → /aˈɸi.i.mi/ → /aˈfi.i.mi/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /a.pʰǐː.ɛː.mi/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /aˈpʰi.e̝.mi/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /aˈɸi.i.mi/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /aˈfi.i.mi/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /aˈfi.i.mi/
