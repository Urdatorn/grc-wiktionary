Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

κατα- (kata-) +‎ ἄγνυμι (ágnumi)

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /ka.tá.ɡnyː.mi/ → /kaˈta.ɣny.mi/ → /kaˈta.ɣni.mi/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /ka.tá.ɡnyː.mi/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /kaˈta.ɡny.mi/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /kaˈta.ɣny.mi/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /kaˈta.ɣny.mi/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /kaˈta.ɣni.mi/

Verb

[edit]

κᾰτάγνῡμῐ • (katágnūmi)

  1. to break in pieces, shatter
  2. to break up, weaken, enervate
