Ancient Greek

[edit]

Alternative forms

[edit]
  • καταδύνω (katadúnō)

Etymology

[edit]

κατα- (kata-) +‎ δύω (dúō)

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /ka.ta.dý.ɔː/ → /ka.taˈðy.o/ → /ka.taˈði.o/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /ka.ta.dý.ɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /ka.taˈdy.o/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /ka.taˈðy.o/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /ka.taˈðy.o/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /ka.taˈði.o/

Verb

[edit]

κᾰτᾰδύω • (katadúō)

  1. to go down into, enter
  2. to cause to sink
  3. to set (of the sun)
  4. to put on
