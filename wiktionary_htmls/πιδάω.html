Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

From the same Pre-Greek root of πῖδαξ (pîdax, “spring, fountain”).

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /piː.dá.ɔː/ → /piˈða.o/ → /piˈða.o/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /piː.dá.ɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /piˈda.o/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /piˈða.o/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /piˈða.o/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /piˈða.o/

Verb

[edit]

πῑδᾰ́ω • (pīdáō)

  1. (transitive) to gush forth
