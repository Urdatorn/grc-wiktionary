Ancient Greek

[edit]

Alternative forms

[edit]
  • κακρῑ́νω (kakrī́nō)

Etymology

[edit]

From κᾰτᾰ- (kata-, “against”) +‎ κρῑ́νω (krī́nō, “to judge”).

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /ka.ta.krǐː.nɔː/ → /ka.taˈkri.no/ → /ka.taˈkri.no/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /ka.ta.krǐː.nɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /ka.taˈkri.no/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /ka.taˈkri.no/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /ka.taˈkri.no/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /ka.taˈkri.no/

Verb

[edit]

κᾰτᾰκρῑ́νω • (katakrī́nō)

  1. to give as a sentence against
    • 436 BCE – 338 BCE, Isocrates, To Demonicus 43
    • 460 BCE – 420 BCE, Herodotus, Histories 7.146
    • 460 BCE – 420 BCE, Herodotus, Histories 2.133
    • 430 BCE – 354 BCE, Xenophon, Apology 7
    1. (with accusative of person) to condemn
      • 480 BCE – 411 BCE, Antiphon of Rhamnus, Third Tetralogy 4.2
      • 460 BCE – 420 BCE, Herodotus, Histories 6.85
      • 460 BCE – 420 BCE, Herodotus, Histories 9.93
      • 300 BCE – 200 BCE, Theocritus, Collected Works 23.23
      • 70 CE – 110 CE, The Gospel of Matthew 20:18
      • 75 CE, Josephus, The Jewish War 5.13.1
      • 384 BCE – 322 BCE, Aristotle, Rhetoric to Alexander 1423B.29
      • 93 CE – 94 CE, Josephus, Antiquities of the Jews 3.14.4
      • 430 BCE – 354 BCE, Xenophon, Hellenica 2.3.54
      • 425 BCE, Euripides, Andromache 496
      • 110 BCE – 35 BCE, Philodemus, Herculaneum Papyri 1251.18
      • 430 BCE – 354 BCE, Xenophon, Hiero 7.10
      • 480 BCE – 411 BCE, Antiphon of Rhamnus, Second Tetralogy 1.1
  2. (passive voice) to be judged or deemed
    • 522 BCE – 443 BCE, Pindar, Fragments 149
