  • IPA(key): /á.ɡa.mai̯/ → /ˈa.ɣa.mɛ/ → /ˈa.ɣa.me/

  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /ˈa.ɣa.me/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /ˈa.ɣa.me/

Verb

[edit]

ἄγᾰμαι • (ágamai)

  1. to wonder at, admire
  2. to be indignant at, resent
