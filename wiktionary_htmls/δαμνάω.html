Ancient Greek

[edit]

Alternative forms

[edit]
  • δαμάζω (damázō)
  • δάμνημι (dámnēmi)

Etymology

[edit]

From Proto-Indo-European *demh₂-. Cognates include Sanskrit दाम्यति (dāmyati), Latin domō, and English tame.

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /dam.ná.ɔː/ → /ðamˈna.o/ → /ðamˈna.o/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /dam.ná.ɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /damˈna.o/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /ðamˈna.o/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /ðamˈna.o/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /ðamˈna.o/

Verb

[edit]

δαμνάω • (damnáō)

  1. Synonym of δαμάζω (damázō)
