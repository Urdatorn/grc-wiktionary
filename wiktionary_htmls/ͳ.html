Ancient Greek

[edit]

Letter

[edit]

ͳ • (š) (upper case Ͳ, modern form ϡ)

  1. Ancient shape of Sampi, an obsolete letter of the Greek alphabet.

Usage notes

[edit]
  • It was used in addition to the classical 24 letters of the alphabet to denote some type of a sibilant sound, probably [ss] or [ts].
