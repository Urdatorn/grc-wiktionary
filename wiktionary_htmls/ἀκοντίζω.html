Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

ᾰ̓́κων (ákōn, “javelin”) +‎ -ῐ́ζω (-ízō)

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /a.kon.tíz.dɔː/ → /a.konˈti.zo/ → /a.konˈdi.zo/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /a.kon.tíz.dɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /a.konˈti.zo/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /a.konˈti.zo/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /a.konˈti.zo/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /a.konˈdi.zo/

Verb

[edit]

ᾰ̓κοντῐ́ζω • (akontízō)

  1. to hurl a javelin
