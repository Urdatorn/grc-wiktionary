Ancient Greek

[edit]

Alternative forms

[edit]
  • πλοΐζομαι (ploḯzomai)

Etymology

[edit]

From the root of πλέω (pléō) + -ίζω (-ízō).

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /plɔː.íz.dɔː/ → /ploˈi.zo/ → /ploˈi.zo/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /plɔː.íz.dɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /ploˈi.zo/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /ploˈi.zo/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /ploˈi.zo/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /ploˈi.zo/

Verb

[edit]

πλωΐζω • (plōḯzō)

  1. to sail on the ocean
