Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

Unexplained.

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /tɔː.tʰáz.dɔː/ → /toˈθa.zo/ → /toˈθa.zo/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /tɔː.tʰáz.dɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /toˈtʰa.zo/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /toˈθa.zo/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /toˈθa.zo/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /toˈθa.zo/

Verb

[edit]

τωθᾰ́ζω • (tōtházō)

  1. (active voice) to mock, jeer at, flout
    Synonyms: σκώπτω (skṓptō), χλευᾰ́ζω (khleuázō)
  2. (passive voice) to be mocked, derided
