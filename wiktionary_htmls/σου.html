  1. unstressed genitive singular of σύ (sú)

Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

From Ancient Greek σοῦ (soû), σύ (sú).

Pronoun

[edit]

σου • (sou) (weak personal genitive form)

  1. (personal, indirect object) you
    Σου είπε την αλήθεια.
    Sou eípe tin alítheia.
    He told you the truth.
  2. (possessive) your
    Ο αδερφός σου ήρθε.
    O aderfós sou írthe.
    Your brother came.

Synonyms

[edit]
  • (personal): εσένα (eséna, strong form)
