Ancient Greek

[edit]

Alternative forms

[edit]
  • ξυγκρούω (xunkroúō) — Old Attic

Etymology

[edit]

From συν- (sun-) +‎ κρούω (kroúō)

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /syŋ.krǔː.ɔː/ → /syŋˈɡru.o/ → /siŋˈɡru.o/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /syŋ.krǔː.ɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /syŋˈkru.o/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /syŋˈɡru.o/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /syŋˈɡru.o/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /siŋˈɡru.o/

Verb

[edit]

συγκρούω • (sunkroúō)

  1. (transitive) to strike together
  2. (transitive) to bring into collision
  3. (intransitive) to clash together, come into collision
