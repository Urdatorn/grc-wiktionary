Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

From περῐ- (peri-, “around”) +‎ βᾰ́λλω (bállō, “to throw”).

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /pe.ri.bál.lɔː/ → /pe.riˈβal.lo/ → /pe.riˈva.lo/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /pe.ri.bál.lɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /pe.riˈbal.lo/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /pe.riˈβal.lo/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /pe.riˈval.lo/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /pe.riˈva.lo/
