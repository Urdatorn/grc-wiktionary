Ancient Greek

[edit]

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /eː.a.ri.nós/ → /i.a.riˈnos/ → /i.a.riˈnos/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /eː.a.ri.nós/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /i.a.riˈnos/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /i.a.riˈnos/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /i.a.riˈnos/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /i.a.riˈnos/

Adjective

[edit]

εἰᾰρῐνός • (eiarinós) m (feminine εἰᾰρῐνή, neuter εἰᾰρῐνόν); first/second declension

  1. Epic form of ἐᾰρῐνός (earinós, “of or in spring”)
