Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]
  • κατα- (kata-) +‎ ἐπείγω (epeígō)

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /ka.te.pěː.ɡɔː/ → /ka.teˈpi.ɣo/ → /ka.teˈpi.ɣo/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /ka.te.pěː.ɡɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /ka.teˈpi.ɡo/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /ka.teˈpi.ɣo/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /ka.teˈpi.ɣo/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /ka.teˈpi.ɣo/

Verb

[edit]

κᾰτεπείγω • (katepeígō)

  1. to press down, depress
  2. to press much, press hard, drive on, urge on, impel
  3. to hasten, make haste
