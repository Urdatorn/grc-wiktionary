Etymology 1

[edit]

From πᾰρᾰ- (para-, “beside”) +‎ εἰμί (eimí, “to be”).

Etymology 2

[edit]

From πᾰρᾰ- (para-, “beside”) +‎ εἶμι (eîmi, “to go”).

Verb

[edit]

πᾰ́ρειμῐ • (páreimi)

  1. to be going to go by, beside, past
Usage notes
[edit]

Functions as the future of παρέρχομαι (parérkhomai).
