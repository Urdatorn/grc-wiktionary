Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

πόνος (pónos) +‎ -έω (-éō)

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /po.né.ɔː/ → /poˈne.o/ → /poˈne.o/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /po.né.ɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /poˈne.o/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /poˈne.o/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /poˈne.o/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /poˈne.o/

Verb

[edit]

πονέω • (ponéō)

  1. (in older Greek exclusively middle voice) to toil, labor, work
