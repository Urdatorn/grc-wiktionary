Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

ἐπι- (epi-) +‎ τρέπω (trépō)

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /e.pi.tré.pɔː/ → /e.piˈtre.po/ → /e.piˈtre.po/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /e.pi.tré.pɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /e.piˈtre.po/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /e.piˈtre.po/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /e.piˈtre.po/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /e.piˈtre.po/

Verb

[edit]

ἐπιτρέπω • (epitrépō)

  1. to entrust, leave (something to someone), turn over (to someone)
  2. to permit, allow
