Ancient Greek

Alternative forms

[edit]
  • μανῐ́ω (maníō) — Doric

Etymology

[edit]

Related to μῆνις.

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /mɛː.ní.ɔː/ → /miˈni.o/ → /miˈni.o/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /mɛː.ní.ɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /me̝ˈni.o/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /miˈni.o/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /miˈni.o/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /miˈni.o/

Verb

[edit]

μηνῐ́ω • (mēníō)

  1. (intransitive, with dative) to be wroth against somebody
  2. (intransitive, with genitive) to be wroth because of something
