Ancient Greek

[edit]

Alternative forms

[edit]
  • βρᾰχέα (brakhéa) — Ionic

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /bra.kʰêː.a/ → /βraˈçi.a/ → /vraˈçi.a/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /bra.kʰêː.a/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /braˈkʰi.a/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /βraˈçi.a/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /vraˈçi.a/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /vraˈçi.a/

Adjective

[edit]

βρᾰχεῖᾰ • (brakheîa)

  1. nominative/vocative feminine singular of βρᾰχύς (brakhús)

Coordinate terms

[edit]
