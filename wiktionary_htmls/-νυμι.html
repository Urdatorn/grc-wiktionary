Ancient Greek

[edit]

Alternative forms

[edit]
  • -ννῡμῐ (-nnūmi) — after a short vowel

Etymology

[edit]

From Proto-Indo-European *-néwti.

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /nyː.mi/ → /ny.mi/ → /ni.mi/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /nyː.mi/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /ny.mi/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /ny.mi/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /ny.mi/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /ni.mi/

Suffix

[edit]

-νῡμῐ • (-nūmi)

  1. A suffix forming transitive verbs
