Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

From the root of μογέω (mogéō).

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /mó.ɡis/ → /ˈmo.ʝis/ → /ˈmo.ʝis/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /mó.ɡis/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /ˈmo.ɡis/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /ˈmo.ʝis/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /ˈmo.ʝis/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /ˈmo.ʝis/

Adverb

[edit]

μόγῐς • (mógis)

  1. with difficulty, hardly, scarcely

Descendants

[edit]
  • →⇒ English: mogigraphia
