Capitalization and punctuation

[edit]
  • r - minuscule (plain)
  • R - majuscule (plain)
  • ʀ - small capital
  • r.
  • R.
  • r-
  • R-
  • R'
  • R!
  • -r
  • (R)
  • ℟ - slashed R

Diacritics and derived forms

[edit]
  • ŕ (with acute)
  • r̀ (with grave)
  • Ř, ř (with hacek)
  • ȑ (with double grave)
  • ȓ (with inverted breve)
  • ɼ (with long leg)
  • ɽ (with tail)
  • ɾ (with fishhook)
  • ɿ (reversed, with descender and fishhook)
  • ṙ (with dot above)
  • ṛ (with dot below)
  • ṝ (with dot below and macron)
  • ṟ (with line below)
  • r̃ (with tilde)
  • ℞ (with crossbar)
