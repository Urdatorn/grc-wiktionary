Ancient Greek

[edit]

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /ez.bi.báz.dɔː/ → /ez.βiˈβa.zo/ → /ez.viˈva.zo/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /ez.bi.báz.dɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /ez.biˈba.zo/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /ez.βiˈβa.zo/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /ez.viˈva.zo/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /ez.viˈva.zo/

Verb

[edit]

ἐσβῐβάζω • (esbibázō)

  1. Old Attic form of εἰσβιβάζω (eisbibázō)
