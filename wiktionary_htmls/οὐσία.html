Ancient Greek

[edit]
FWOTD – 6 November 2014

Alternative forms

[edit]
  • οὐσίη (ousíē) — Ionic
  • ἐσσία (essía), ὠσία (ōsía) — Doric

Etymology

[edit]

From ὤν, οὖσᾰ, ὄν (ṓn, oûsa, ón, “being”), the present participle of εἰμῐ́ (eimí) (“to be”), + -ῐ́ᾱ (-íā, abstract noun suffix).[1]
