Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

From ἀ- (a-, “not”) +‎ ὁρῐστός (horistós, “limited”).

Adjective

[edit]

ᾰ̓όρῐστος • (aóristos) m or f (neuter ᾰ̓όρῐστον); second declension

  1. indefinite, indeterminate
  2. without boundaries, debatable
  3. the phrase ὁ ἀόριστος (χρόνος) (ho aóristos (khrónos)): the aorist tense
