Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

From Proto-Indo-European *steh₂-.

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /stá.di.os/ → /ˈsta.ði.os/ → /ˈsta.ði.os/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /stá.di.os/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /ˈsta.di.os/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /ˈsta.ði.os/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /ˈsta.ði.os/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /ˈsta.ði.os/

Adjective

[edit]

στᾰ́δῐος • (stádios) m (feminine στᾰδῐ́ᾱ, neuter στᾰ́δῐον); first/second declension

  1. standing fast and firm
  2. firm, fixed, immobile
  3. standing upright or straight
