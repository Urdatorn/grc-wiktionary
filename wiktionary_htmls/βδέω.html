Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

From Proto-Hellenic *bzdḗō, from Proto-Indo-European *psdéh₁yeti, from *pesd- (“to fart”).

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /bdé.ɔː/ → /ˈβðe.o/ → /ˈvðe.o/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /bdé.ɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /ˈbde.o/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /ˈβðe.o/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /ˈvðe.o/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /ˈvðe.o/

Verb

[edit]

βδέω • (bdéō)

  1. to break wind, fart
