Ancient Greek

[edit]

Alternative forms

[edit]
  • ὑπολιμπάνω (hupolimpánō)

Etymology

[edit]

ὑπο- (hupo-) +‎ λείπω (leípō)

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /hy.po.lěː.pɔː/ → /y.poˈli.po/ → /i.poˈli.po/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /hy.po.lěː.pɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /(h)y.poˈli.po/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /y.poˈli.po/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /y.poˈli.po/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /i.poˈli.po/

Verb

[edit]

ὑπολείπω • (hupoleípō)

  1. to leave over, leave behind
  2. (in middle) to remain
