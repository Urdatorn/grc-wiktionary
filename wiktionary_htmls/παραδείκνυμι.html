Ancient Greek

[edit]

Alternative forms

[edit]
  • παραδεικνύω (paradeiknúō) — Koine

Etymology

[edit]

From παρα- (para-, “beside, near”) +‎ δείκνυμι (deíknumi, “to show”).

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /pa.ra.děː.knyː.mi/ → /pa.raˈði.kny.mi/ → /pa.raˈði.kni.mi/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /pa.ra.děː.knyː.mi/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /pa.raˈdi.kny.mi/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /pa.raˈði.kny.mi/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /pa.raˈði.kny.mi/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /pa.raˈði.kni.mi/

Verb

[edit]

πᾰρᾰδείκνῡμῐ • (paradeíknūmi)

  1. to hold up to viewing, exhibit, point out
  2. to set as an example or model
  3. to compare
  4. to demonstrate
