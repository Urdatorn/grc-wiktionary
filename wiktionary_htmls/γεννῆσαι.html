Ancient Greek

[edit]

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /ɡen.nɛ̂ː.sai̯/ → /ʝenˈni.sɛ/ → /ʝeˈni.se/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /ɡen.nɛ̂ː.sai̯/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /ɡenˈne̝.sɛ/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /ʝenˈni.sɛ/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /ʝenˈni.se/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /ʝeˈni.se/

Verb

[edit]

γεννῆσαι • (gennêsai)

  1. aorist active infinitive of γεννᾰ́ω (gennáō)
