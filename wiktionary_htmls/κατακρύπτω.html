Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

From κατα- (kata-, “intensifying prefix”) +‎ κρύπτω (krúptō, “hide”)

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /ka.ta.krýp.tɔː/ → /ka.taˈkryp.to/ → /ka.taˈkrip.to/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /ka.ta.krýp.tɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /ka.taˈkryp.to/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /ka.taˈkryp.to/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /ka.taˈkryp.to/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /ka.taˈkrip.to/

Verb

[edit]

κᾰτᾰκρύπτω • (katakrúptō)

  1. to hide, conceal
  2. (euphemistic) to put away
  3. to use concealment, conceal oneself or one's true nature
