Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

ἀπο- (apo-) +‎ ἄγω (ágō)

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /a.pá.ɡɔː/ → /aˈpa.ɣo/ → /aˈpa.ɣo/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /a.pá.ɡɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /aˈpa.ɡo/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /aˈpa.ɣo/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /aˈpa.ɣo/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /aˈpa.ɣo/

Verb

[edit]

ἀπάγω • (apágō)

  1. to lead away
  2. to carry off, abduct, kidnap
  3. to retire, withdraw, march away
  4. to bring back, bring home
  5. to pay back
