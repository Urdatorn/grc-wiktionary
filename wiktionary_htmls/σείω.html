Ancient Greek

[edit]

Alternative forms

[edit]
  • σίω (síō) — Poetic

Etymology

[edit]

From Proto-Hellenic *tséihō, from Proto-Indo-European *twéys-e-ti, from *tweys-. Cognates include Sanskrit त्वेषति (tvéṣati), Avestan 𐬚𐬬𐬀𐬫𐬀𐬵 (θvayah, “fright, fear”).
