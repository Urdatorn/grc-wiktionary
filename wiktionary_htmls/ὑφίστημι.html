Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

From ῠ̔πο- (hupo-, “under”) +‎ ῐ̔́στημῐ (hístēmi, “to stand”).

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /hy.pʰís.tɛː.mi/ → /yˈɸis.ti.mi/ → /iˈfis.ti.mi/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /hy.pʰís.tɛː.mi/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /(h)yˈpʰis.te̝.mi/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /yˈɸis.ti.mi/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /yˈfis.ti.mi/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /iˈfis.ti.mi/

Verb

[edit]

ῠ̔φῐ́στημῐ • (huphístēmi)

  1. to stand under
