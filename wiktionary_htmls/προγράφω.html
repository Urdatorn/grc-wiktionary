Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

προ- (pro-) +‎ γράφω (gráphō)

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /pro.ɡrá.pʰɔː/ → /proˈɣra.ɸo/ → /proˈɣra.fo/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /pro.ɡrá.pʰɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /proˈɡra.pʰo/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /proˈɣra.ɸo/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /proˈɣra.fo/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /proˈɣra.fo/

Verb

[edit]

προγράφω • (prográphō)

  1. to write before or first
  2. to give public notice of, set forth publicly
  3. to summon by public notice
  4. to write at the top
