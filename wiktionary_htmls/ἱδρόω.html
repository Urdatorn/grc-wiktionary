Ancient Greek

[edit]

Alternative forms

[edit]
  • ἱδρώσσω (hidrṓssō)
  • ἱδρώττω (hidrṓttō) — Attic

Etymology

[edit]

From ἱδρώς (hidrṓs, “sweat”) +‎ -όω (-óō).

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /hi.dró.ɔː/ → /iˈðro.o/ → /iˈðro.o/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /hi.dró.ɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /(h)iˈdro.o/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /iˈðro.o/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /iˈðro.o/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /iˈðro.o/

Verb

[edit]

ἱδρόω • (hidróō)

  1. to sweat, perspire
    • 384 BCE – 322 BCE, Aristotle, Problems 867b34
    • 800 BCE – 600 BCE, Homer, Iliad 2.388
    • 800 BCE – 600 BCE, Homer, Iliad 4.27
    • 800 BCE – 600 BCE, Homer, Iliad 11.119
    • 800 BCE – 600 BCE, Homer, Iliad 18.372
    • 800 BCE – 600 BCE, Homer, Odyssey 4.39
