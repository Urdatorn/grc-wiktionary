Etymology

[edit]

τετρα- (tetra-) +‎ -ακόσιοι (-akósioi)

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /te.tra.kó.si.oi̯/ → /te.traˈko.si.y/ → /te.traˈko.si.i/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /te.tra.kó.si.oi̯/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /te.traˈko.si.y/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /te.traˈko.si.y/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /te.traˈko.si.y/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /te.traˈko.si.i/

Numeral

[edit]

τετρᾰκόσῐοι • (tetrakósioi)

  1. four hundred
