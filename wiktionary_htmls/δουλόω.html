Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

δοῦλος (doûlos, “slave”) +‎ -όω (-óō)

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /duː.ló.ɔː/ → /ðuˈlo.o/ → /ðuˈlo.o/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /duː.ló.ɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /duˈlo.o/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /ðuˈlo.o/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /ðuˈlo.o/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /ðuˈlo.o/

Verb

[edit]

δουλόω • (doulóō)

  1. to enslave
