Ancient Greek

[edit]

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /ksy.nǐː.ɛː.mi/ → /ksyˈni.i.mi/ → /ksiˈni.i.mi/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /ksy.nǐː.ɛː.mi/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /ksyˈni.e̝.mi/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /ksyˈni.i.mi/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /ksyˈni.i.mi/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /ksiˈni.i.mi/

Verb

[edit]

ξῠνῑ́ημῐ • (xunī́ēmi)

  1. Old Attic form of συνίημι (suníēmi)
