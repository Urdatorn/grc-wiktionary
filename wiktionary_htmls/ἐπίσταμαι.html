Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

From ἐπι- (epi-) +‎ ἵστημι (hístēmi). Compare ἐφῐ́στημῐ (ephístēmi).

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /e.pís.ta.mai̯/ → /eˈpis.ta.mɛ/ → /eˈpis.ta.me/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /e.pís.ta.mai̯/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /eˈpis.ta.mɛ/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /eˈpis.ta.mɛ/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /eˈpis.ta.me/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /eˈpis.ta.me/

Verb

[edit]

ἐπῐ́στᾰμαι • (epístamai)

  1. (transitive) to know, to have the knowledge of something
