Ancient Greek

[edit]

Alternative forms

[edit]
  • ᾰνᾰγῑνώσκω (anagīnṓskō) — Ionic, Koine

Etymology

[edit]

From ᾰ̓νᾰ- (ana-, “up”) +‎ γῐγνώσκω (gignṓskō, “to recognize”).

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /a.na.ɡi.ɡnɔ̌ːs.kɔː/ → /a.na.ʝiˈɣnos.ko/ → /a.na.ʝiˈɣnos.ko/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /a.na.ɡi.ɡnɔ̌ːs.kɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /a.na.ɡiˈɡnos.ko/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /a.na.ʝiˈɣnos.ko/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /a.na.ʝiˈɣnos.ko/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /a.na.ʝiˈɣnos.ko/

Verb

[edit]

ᾰ̓νᾰγῐγνώσκω • (anagignṓskō)

  1. (Epic, in the second aorist)
    1. (transitive) to know well, know certainly
      • 800 BCE – 600 BCE, Homer, Odyssey 1.216
      • 800 BCE – 600 BCE, Homer, Odyssey 21.205
      • 800 BCE – 600 BCE, Homer, Iliad 13.734
    2. (transitive) to know again, recognize
      • 800 BCE – 600 BCE, Homer, Odyssey 4.250
      • 460 BCE – 420 BCE, Herodotus, Histories 2.91
      • 522 BCE – 443 BCE, Pindar, Isthmian Ode 2.23
      • 412 BCE, Euripides, Helen
  2. (Attic, of written characters, transitive) to know them again, to read
    • 445 BCE – 380 BCE, Lysias, On the Property of Eraton 9
    • 386 BCE – 367 BCE, Plato, Parmenides 127D
    • 436 BCE – 338 BCE, Isocrates, Antidosis 67
    • 522 BCE – 443 BCE, Pindar, Olympian Ode 10.1
    • 384 BCE – 322 BCE, Demosthenes, Against Leptines 27
    • 330 BCE, Demosthenes, On the Crown 118
    • 343 BCE, Demosthenes, On the False Embassy 70
    • 384 BCE – 322 BCE, Demosthenes, Against Leochares 45
    • 405 BCE, Aristophanes, The Frogs 52
    • 46 CE – 120 CE, Plutarch, Alexander the Great 1
    • 3rd century CE, Diogenes Laertius, Lives and Opinions of Eminent Philosophers 5.73
  3. (Ionic, causative, mostly in aorist, transitive) to persuade or induce one to do something
    • 460 BCE – 420 BCE, Herodotus, Histories 5.106
    • 460 BCE – 420 BCE, Herodotus, Histories 1.87
    • 460 BCE – 420 BCE, Herodotus, Histories 4.158
    • 460 BCE – 420 BCE, Herodotus, Histories 6.83
    • 460 BCE – 420 BCE, Herodotus, Histories 1.68
    • 460 BCE – 420 BCE, Herodotus, Histories 7.10
    • 460 BCE – 370 BCE, Hippocrates, On Joints 1
    • 460 BCE – 420 BCE, Herodotus, Histories 7.7
    • 460 BCE – 420 BCE, Herodotus, Histories 4.154
    • 460 BCE – 420 BCE, Herodotus, Histories 6.50
    • 460 BCE – 420 BCE, Herodotus, Histories 8.110
    • 480 BCE – 411 BCE, Antiphon of Rhamnus, First Tetralogy 2.7
