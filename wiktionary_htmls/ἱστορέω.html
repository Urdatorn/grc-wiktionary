Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

From ἵστωρ (hístōr).

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /his.to.ré.ɔː/ → /is.toˈre.o/ → /is.toˈre.o/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /his.to.ré.ɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /(h)is.toˈre.o/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /is.toˈre.o/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /is.toˈre.o/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /is.toˈre.o/

Verb

[edit]

ῐ̔στορέω • (historéō)

  1. to inquire, ask (of someone, or regarding someone/something)
  2. to examine, observe
  3. to record, give an account of what one has learned
