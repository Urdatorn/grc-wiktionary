Ancient Greek

[edit]

Alternative forms

[edit]
  • ὀμίλλω (omíllō) — Aeolic

Etymology

[edit]

From ὅμῑλος (hómīlos, “crowd”) +‎ -έω (-éō).

Verb

[edit]

ὁμῑλέω • (homīléō)

  1. to be with, keep company
  2. (Byzantine) to talk, converse
