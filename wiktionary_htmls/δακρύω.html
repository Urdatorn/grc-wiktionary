Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

From δάκρυ (dákru).

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /da.kry̌ː.ɔː/ → /ðaˈkry.o/ → /ðaˈkri.o/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /da.kry̌ː.ɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /daˈkry.o/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /ðaˈkry.o/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /ðaˈkry.o/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /ðaˈkri.o/

Verb

[edit]

δακρῡ́ω • (dakrū́ō)

  1. to cry, weep, shed tears
  2. (of eyes) to run
  3. (of trees) to exude tree sap
  4. to cry, weep for
