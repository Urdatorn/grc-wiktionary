Ancient Greek

[edit]

Alternative forms

[edit]
  • κρετέω (kretéō) — Aeolic

Etymology

[edit]

From κράτος (krátos, “power, might”) +‎ -έω (-éō, denominative verbal suffix).

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /kra.té.ɔː/ → /kraˈte.o/ → /kraˈte.o/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /kra.té.ɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /kraˈte.o/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /kraˈte.o/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /kraˈte.o/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /kraˈte.o/

Verb

[edit]

κρατέω • (kratéō)

  1. to rule, command
  2. to conquer, prevail, gain the upper hand
  3. to seize, hold
