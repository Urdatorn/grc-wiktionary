Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

From δια- (dia-) +‎ σκοπέω (skopéō).

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /di.as.ko.pé.ɔː/ → /ði.as.koˈpe.o/ → /ði.as.koˈpe.o/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /di.as.ko.pé.ɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /di.as.koˈpe.o/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /ði.as.koˈpe.o/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /ði.as.koˈpe.o/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /ði.as.koˈpe.o/

Verb

[edit]

δῐασκοπέω • (diaskopéō)

  1. to look at in different ways, to examine or consider
  2. to keep watching
