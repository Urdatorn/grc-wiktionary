Ancient Greek

[edit]

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /é.kʰɔːn/ → /ˈe.xon/ → /ˈe.xon/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /é.kʰɔːn/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /ˈe.kʰon/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /ˈe.xon/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /ˈe.xon/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /ˈe.xon/

Participle

[edit]

ἔχων • (ékhōn) m (feminine ἔχουσᾰ, neuter ἔχον); first/third declension

  1. present active participle of ἔχω (ékhō)
