Ancient Greek

[edit]

Alternative forms

[edit]
  • μύζω (múzō)
  • μυζέω (muzéō)

Etymology

[edit]

Onomatopoeic.[1]

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /myz.dá.ɔː/ → /myˈza.o/ → /miˈza.o/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /myz.dá.ɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /myˈza.o/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /myˈza.o/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /myˈza.o/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /miˈza.o/

Verb

[edit]

μυζᾰ́ω • (muzáō)

  1. to suck
