Ancient Greek

[edit]

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /pro.miˈθe.βo.mɛ/ → /pro.miˈθe.vo.me/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /pro.me̝ˈtʰe.wo.mɛ/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /pro.miˈθe.βo.mɛ/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /pro.miˈθe.vo.me/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /pro.miˈθe.vo.me/

Verb

[edit]

προμηθεύομαι • (promētheúomai)

  1. Koine form of προμηθέομαι (promēthéomai, “use forethought, take care”)

Greek

[edit]

Pronunciation

[edit]
  • IPA(key): /pɾo.miˈθe.vo.me/
  • Hyphenation: προ‧μη‧θεύ‧ο‧μαι

Verb

[edit]

προμηθεύομαι • (promithévomai) passive (past προμηθεύθηκα/προμηθεύτηκα, active προμηθεύω)

  1. to be provided, supplied
