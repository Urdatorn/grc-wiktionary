Ancient Greek

[edit]

Alternative forms

[edit]
  • κᾰθερῐ́ζω (katherízō)

Etymology

[edit]

From κᾰθᾰρός (katharós, “clean, pure”) +‎ -ίζω (-ízō).

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /ka.tʰa.ríz.dɔː/ → /ka.θaˈri.zo/ → /ka.θaˈri.zo/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /ka.tʰa.ríz.dɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /ka.tʰaˈri.zo/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /ka.θaˈri.zo/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /ka.θaˈri.zo/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /ka.θaˈri.zo/

Verb

[edit]

κᾰθᾰρῐ́ζω • (katharízō)

  1. to clean, cleanse, purge, purify

Greek

[edit]

Pronunciation

[edit]
  • IPA(key): /ka.θaˈɾi.zo/

Verb

[edit]

καθαρίζω • (katharízo) (past καθάρισα, passive καθαρίζομαι)

  1. to clean, to wash
  2. to peel, to descale
  3. to cleanse, to purify
  4. (slang) to kill
