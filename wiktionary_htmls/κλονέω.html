Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

κλόνος (klónos) +‎ -έω (-éō)

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /klo.né.ɔː/ → /kloˈne.o/ → /kloˈne.o/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /klo.né.ɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /kloˈne.o/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /kloˈne.o/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /kloˈne.o/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /kloˈne.o/

Verb

[edit]

κλονέω • (klonéō)

  1. to drive in confusion
  2. to cause to rush wildly
  3. to ruffle
