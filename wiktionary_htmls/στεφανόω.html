Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

στέφανος (stéphanos, “crown, wreath”) +‎ -όω (-óō, denominal verb suffix)

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /ste.pʰa.nó.ɔː/ → /ste.ɸaˈno.o/ → /ste.faˈno.o/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /ste.pʰa.nó.ɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /ste.pʰaˈno.o/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /ste.ɸaˈno.o/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /ste.faˈno.o/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /ste.faˈno.o/

Verb

[edit]

στεφανόω • (stephanóō)

  1. to crown, adorn, honour
  2. (passive voice) to be put round in a circle
