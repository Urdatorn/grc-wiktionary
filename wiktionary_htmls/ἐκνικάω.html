Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

ἐκ- (ek-) +‎ νῑκάω (nīkáō)

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /e.kniː.ká.ɔː/ → /e.kniˈka.o/ → /e.kniˈka.o/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /e.kniː.ká.ɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /e.kniˈka.o/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /e.kniˈka.o/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /e.kniˈka.o/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /e.kniˈka.o/

Verb

[edit]

ἐκνῑκάω • (eknīkáō)

  1. to achieve by force
