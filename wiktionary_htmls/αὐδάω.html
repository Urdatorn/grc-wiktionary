Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

αὐδή (audḗ, “human voice”) +‎ -άω (-áō, verb suffix)

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /au̯.dá.ɔː/ → /aβˈða.o/ → /avˈða.o/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /au̯.dá.ɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /awˈda.o/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /aβˈða.o/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /avˈða.o/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /avˈða.o/

Verb

[edit]

αὐδάω • (audáō)

  1. to speak, utter
  2. to speak to, address
