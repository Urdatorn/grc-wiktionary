Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /dɛː.tʰy̌ː.nɔː/ → /ðiˈθy.no/ → /ðiˈθi.no/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /dɛː.tʰy̌ː.nɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /de̝ˈtʰy.no/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /ðiˈθy.no/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /ðiˈθy.no/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /ðiˈθi.no/

Verb

[edit]

δηθῡ́νω • (dēthū́nō)

  1. (Epic) to tarry, delay
