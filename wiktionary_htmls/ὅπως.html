Ancient Greek

[edit]

Alternative forms

[edit]
  • ὅππως (hóppōs) — Epic, Aeolic
  • ὅκως (hókōs) — Ionic

Etymology

[edit]

From ὅς (hós, “who, which”) + πῶς (pôs, “how”).

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /hó.pɔːs/ → /ˈo.pos/ → /ˈo.pos/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /hó.pɔːs/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /ˈ(h)o.pos/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /ˈo.pos/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /ˈo.pos/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /ˈo.pos/
