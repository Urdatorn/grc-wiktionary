Ancient Greek

[edit]

Alternative forms

[edit]
  • κάρυον Ποντικόν (káruon Pontikón)

Etymology

[edit]

Ποντικόν (Pontikón, “Pontic”) κάρυον (káruon, “nut”). From the nut's indigenousness and abundance in Pontus, a former Hellenic kingdom in northern Anatolia along the southern shore of the Black Sea.

Noun

[edit]

Ποντικόν κάρυον • (Pontikón káruon)

  1. hazelnut

Synonyms

[edit]
  • καρύα Ποντικά (karúa Pontiká)
