Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

From ὑπο- (hupo-) +‎ δείδω (deídō)

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /hy.po.děː.dɔː/ → /y.poˈði.ðo/ → /i.poˈði.ðo/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /hy.po.děː.dɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /(h)y.poˈdi.do/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /y.poˈði.ðo/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /y.poˈði.ðo/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /i.poˈði.ðo/

Verb

[edit]

ὑποδείδω • (hupodeídō)

  1. (transitive) to cower under or before
  2. (transitive) to fear (secretly)
