Ancient Greek

[edit]

Alternative forms

[edit]
  • ἀνίσχω (anískhō)

Etymology

[edit]

ἀνα- (ana-) +‎ ἔχω (ékhō)

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /a.né.kʰɔː/ → /aˈne.xo/ → /aˈne.xo/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /a.né.kʰɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /aˈne.kʰo/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /aˈne.xo/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /aˈne.xo/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /aˈne.xo/

Verb

[edit]

ἀνέχω • (anékhō)

  1. to hold up one's hands (in fight, in surrender, in prayer etc.)
  2. to make ready
  3. to lift up, exalt
  4. to uphold, maintain
  5. to put forth
  6. to hold back
  7. to rise up, emerge
  8. to arise, happen
  9. to appear, show oneself
  10. to project, jut out
  11. to hold on, keep doing (+ participle)
  12. to cease
  13. to wait, delay
  14. to be alleviated of pain
