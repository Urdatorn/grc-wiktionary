Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

From προσ- (pros-, “toward”) +‎ διαλῡ́ω (dialū́ō, “dissolve”)

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /proz.di.a.ly̌ː.ɔː/ → /proz.ði.aˈly.o/ → /proz.ði.aˈli.o/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /proz.di.a.ly̌ː.ɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /proz.di.aˈly.o/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /proz.ði.aˈly.o/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /proz.ði.aˈly.o/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /proz.ði.aˈli.o/

Verb

[edit]

προσδῐᾰλῡ́ω • (prosdialū́ō)

  1. to relax further
