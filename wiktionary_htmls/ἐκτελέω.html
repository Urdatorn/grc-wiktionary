Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

From ἐκ- (ek-) +‎ τελέω (teléō)

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /ek.te.lé.ɔː/ → /ek.teˈle.o/ → /ek.teˈle.o/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /ek.te.lé.ɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /ek.teˈle.o/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /ek.teˈle.o/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /ek.teˈle.o/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /ek.teˈle.o/

Verb

[edit]

ἐκτελέω • (ekteléō)

  1. to bring to an end, finish
  2. to accomplish, achieve
