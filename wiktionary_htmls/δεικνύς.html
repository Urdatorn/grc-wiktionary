Ancient Greek

[edit]

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /deː.kny̌ːs/ → /ðiˈknys/ → /ðiˈknis/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /deː.kny̌ːs/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /diˈknys/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /ðiˈknys/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /ðiˈknys/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /ðiˈknis/

Verb

[edit]

δεικνῡ́ς • (deiknū́s)

  1. present active participle of δείκνυμι (deíknumi)
