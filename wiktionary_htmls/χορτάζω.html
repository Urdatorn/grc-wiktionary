Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

From χόρτος (khórtos, “fodder”) +‎ -άζω (-ázō, suffix to form a verb).

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /kʰor.táz.dɔː/ → /xorˈta.zo/ → /xorˈta.zo/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /kʰor.táz.dɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /kʰorˈta.zo/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /xorˈta.zo/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /xorˈta.zo/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /xorˈta.zo/

Verb

[edit]

χορτᾰ́ζω • (khortázō)

  1. (of cattle) to feed, fatten
  2. (of persons) to feed, fill full of
