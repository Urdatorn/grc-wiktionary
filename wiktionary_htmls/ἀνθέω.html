Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

ἄνθος (ánthos) +‎ -έω (-éō)

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /an.tʰé.ɔː/ → /anˈθe.o/ → /anˈθe.o/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /an.tʰé.ɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /anˈtʰe.o/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /anˈθe.o/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /anˈθe.o/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /anˈθe.o/

Verb

[edit]

ἀνθέω • (anthéō)

  1. to flower, blossom
