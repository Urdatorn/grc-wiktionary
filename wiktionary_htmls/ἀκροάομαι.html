Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

ἀκροαομαι < the main theory is that it comes from ἀκρ- (ἄκρ-ος, ἀκ-μή) + οὖς ("ear")

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /a.kro.á.o.ma.i/ → /a.kroˈa.o.ma.i/ → /a.kroˈa.o.ma.i/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /a.kro.á.o.ma.i/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /a.kroˈa.o.ma.i/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /a.kroˈa.o.ma.i/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /a.kroˈa.o.ma.i/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /a.kroˈa.o.ma.i/

Verb

[edit]

ἀκροάομαι • (akroáomai)

  1. (middle only) to listen
