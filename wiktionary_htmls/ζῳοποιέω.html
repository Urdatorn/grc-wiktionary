Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

ζῷον (zôion) +‎ ποιέω (poiéō)

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /zdɔːi̯.o.poi̯.é.ɔː/ → /zo.o.pyˈe.o/ → /zo.o.piˈe.o/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /zdɔːi̯.o.poi̯.é.ɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /zo.o.pyˈe.o/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /zo.o.pyˈe.o/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /zo.o.pyˈe.o/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /zo.o.piˈe.o/

Verb

[edit]

ζῳοποιέω • (zōiopoiéō)

  1. to make life; to give life
  2. to quicken
  3. to vivify, to animate
