Noun

[edit]

σιρομάστης • (siromástēs)

  1. pit-searcher, i.e. a probe or gauge, with which tax-gatherers searched corn-pits and magazines, used in war to try whether there were pits in the ground
  2. barbed lance of the same shape
    • 300 BCE – 200 BCE, Septuagint, 1 Kings 18.28
