Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

ἐν- (en-) +‎ καθίστημι (kathístēmi)

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /eŋ.ka.tʰís.tɛː.mi/ → /eŋ.ɡaˈθis.ti.mi/ → /eŋ.ɡaˈθis.ti.mi/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /eŋ.ka.tʰís.tɛː.mi/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /eŋ.kaˈtʰis.te̝.mi/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /eŋ.ɡaˈθis.ti.mi/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /eŋ.ɡaˈθis.ti.mi/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /eŋ.ɡaˈθis.ti.mi/

Verb

[edit]

ἐγκᾰθίστημῐ • (enkathístēmi)

  1. to place in, establish in
