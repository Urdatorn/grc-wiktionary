Ancient Greek

[edit]

Alternative forms

[edit]
  • ξυνοικίζω (xunoikízō) — Old Attic

Etymology

[edit]

συν- (sun-) +‎ οἰκίζω (oikízō)

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /sy.noi̯.kíz.dɔː/ → /sy.nyˈci.zo/ → /si.niˈci.zo/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /sy.noi̯.kíz.dɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /sy.nyˈki.zo/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /sy.nyˈci.zo/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /sy.nyˈci.zo/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /si.niˈci.zo/

Verb

[edit]

σῠνοικίζω • (sunoikízō)

  1. to make (someone) live with (someone else)
  2. to give (someone to someone) in marriage
  3. to combine or join in one city
  4. to unite into a city-state
  5. to unite in one building
  6. to join in peopling or colonizing a country
  7. to unite, associate
