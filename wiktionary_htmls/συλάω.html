Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

σῡ́λη (sū́lē) +‎ -άω (-áō)

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /syː.lá.ɔː/ → /syˈla.o/ → /siˈla.o/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /syː.lá.ɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /syˈla.o/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /syˈla.o/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /syˈla.o/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /siˈla.o/

Verb

[edit]

σῡλάω • (sūláō)

  1. to pillage, plunder, rob, despoil
    Synonyms: πορθέω (porthéō), σῑ́νομαι (sī́nomai), σκῡλεύω (skūleúō)
