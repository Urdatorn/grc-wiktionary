Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

From ἀπο- (apo-) +‎ θεός (theós, “god, deity”) +‎ -όω (-óō).

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /a.po.tʰe.ó.ɔː/ → /a.po.θeˈo.o/ → /a.po.θeˈo.o/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /a.po.tʰe.ó.ɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /a.po.tʰeˈo.o/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /a.po.θeˈo.o/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /a.po.θeˈo.o/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /a.po.θeˈo.o/

Verb

[edit]

ἀποθεόω • (apotheóō)

  1. to deify
