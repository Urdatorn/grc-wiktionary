Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

λεπτός (leptós) +‎ -ύνω (-únō).

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /lep.ty̌ː.nɔː/ → /lepˈty.no/ → /lepˈti.no/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /lep.ty̌ː.nɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /lepˈty.no/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /lepˈty.no/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /lepˈty.no/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /lepˈti.no/

Verb

[edit]

λεπτύ̄νω • (leptú̄nō)

  1. to make thin or meager
  2. to comminute or liquefy food in digestion
  3. to thresh, winnow
