Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

ἐρῆμος (erêmos) +‎ -όω (-óō)

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /e.rɛː.mó.ɔː/ → /e.riˈmo.o/ → /e.riˈmo.o/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /e.rɛː.mó.ɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /e.re̝ˈmo.o/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /e.riˈmo.o/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /e.riˈmo.o/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /e.riˈmo.o/

Verb

[edit]

ἐρημόω • (erēmóō)

  1. to lay waste, devastate, bring to desolation, bring to nought
