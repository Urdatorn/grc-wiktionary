Ancient Greek

[edit]

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /ksym.pro.pém.pɔː/ → /ksym.proˈpem.po/ → /ksim.broˈpem.bo/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /ksym.pro.pém.pɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /ksym.proˈpem.po/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /ksym.proˈpem.po/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /ksym.proˈpem.po/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /ksim.broˈpem.bo/

Verb

[edit]

ξυμπροπέμπω • (xumpropémpō)

  1. Old Attic form of συμπροπέμπω (sumpropémpō)
