Ancient Greek

[edit]

Alternative forms

[edit]
  • ἀποτελῶ (apotelô) — contracted

Etymology

[edit]

From ἀπό (apó) +‎ τελέω (teléō).

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /a.po.te.lé.ɔː/ → /a.po.teˈle.o/ → /a.po.teˈle.o/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /a.po.te.lé.ɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /a.po.teˈle.o/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /a.po.teˈle.o/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /a.po.teˈle.o/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /a.po.teˈle.o/

Verb

[edit]

ᾰ̓ποτελέω • (apoteléō)

  1. (transitive) to finish (something), to accomplish, to form fully
