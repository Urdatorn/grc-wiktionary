Ancient Greek

[edit]

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /es.plé.ɔː/ → /esˈple.o/ → /esˈple.o/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /es.plé.ɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /esˈple.o/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /esˈple.o/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /esˈple.o/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /esˈple.o/

Verb

[edit]

ἐσπλέω • (espléō)

  1. Old Attic and Ionic form of εἰσπλέω (eispléō)
