Ancient Greek

[edit]

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /é.ni.oi̯/ → /ˈe.ni.y/ → /ˈe.ni.i/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /é.ni.oi̯/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /ˈe.ni.y/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /ˈe.ni.y/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /ˈe.ni.y/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /ˈe.ni.i/

Determiner

[edit]

ἔνῐοι • (énioi) (indefinite, plural only, feminine ἔνῐαι, neuter ἔνῐᾰ)

  1. some
