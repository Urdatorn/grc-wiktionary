Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

From παρα- (para-, “beside, contrary to”) +‎ κρύπτω (krúptō, “hide”)

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /pa.ra.krýp.tɔː/ → /pa.raˈkryp.to/ → /pa.raˈkrip.to/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /pa.ra.krýp.tɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /pa.raˈkryp.to/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /pa.raˈkryp.to/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /pa.raˈkryp.to/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /pa.raˈkrip.to/

Verb

[edit]

πᾰρᾰκρύπτω • (parakrúptō)

  1. to hide oneself or one's sympathies
