Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

From κατα- (kata-) +‎ βοάω (boáō).

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /ka.ta.bo.á.ɔː/ → /ka.ta.βoˈa.o/ → /ka.ta.voˈa.o/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /ka.ta.bo.á.ɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /ka.ta.boˈa.o/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /ka.ta.βoˈa.o/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /ka.ta.voˈa.o/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /ka.ta.voˈa.o/

Verb

[edit]

κᾰτᾰβοάω • (kataboáō)

  1. to cry down, cry out against [with genitive]
  2. (transitive) to bawl down, outcry
