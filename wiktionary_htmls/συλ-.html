Ancient Greek

[edit]

Prefix

[edit]

σῠλ- • (sul-) occurs before λ (l)

  1. Alternative form of σῠν- (sun-)

Greek

[edit]

Prefix

[edit]

συλ- • (syl-) or σύλ-

  1. Alternative form of συν- (syn-) occurs before the consonant λ (l)
    συν- (“plus”) + λαμβάνω (“receive”) → συλλαμβάνω (syllamváno, “arrest”) /si.lamˈva.no/
    the two λλ are pronounced as one
