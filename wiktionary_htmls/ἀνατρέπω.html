Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

From ἀνα- (ana-) +‎ τρέπω (trépō)

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /a.na.tré.pɔː/ → /a.naˈtre.po/ → /a.naˈtre.po/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /a.na.tré.pɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /a.naˈtre.po/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /a.naˈtre.po/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /a.naˈtre.po/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /a.naˈtre.po/

Verb

[edit]

ἀνατρέπω • (anatrépō)

  1. to turn up, turn over, overturn, upset
  2. to overthrow, subvert
  3. to upset in argument, refute
  4. to dishearten
  5. to stir up, awaken
