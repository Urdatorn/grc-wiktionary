Ancient Greek

[edit]

Alternative forms

[edit]
  • ναίω (naíō)

Etymology

[edit]

From Proto-Indo-European *(s)neh₂- (“to flow, to swim”). Cognate with Ναϊάς (Naïás), Νηρεύς (Nēreús), Latin nō, Sanskrit स्नौति (snauti, “to flow; drip”).

Verb

[edit]

νᾱ́ω • (nā́ō)

  1. to flow (over)
