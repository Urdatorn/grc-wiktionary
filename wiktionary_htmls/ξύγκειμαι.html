Ancient Greek

[edit]

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /ksýŋ.keː.mai̯/ → /ˈksyɲ.ɟi.mɛ/ → /ˈksiɲ.ɟi.me/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /ksýŋ.keː.mai̯/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /ˈksyŋ.ki.mɛ/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /ˈksyɲ.ɟi.mɛ/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /ˈksyɲ.ɟi.me/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /ˈksiɲ.ɟi.me/

Verb

[edit]

ξύγκειμαι • (xúnkeimai)

  1. Old Attic form of σύγκειμαι (súnkeimai)
