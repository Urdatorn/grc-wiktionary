Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

From ἐκ- (ek-) +‎ μάσσω (mássō)

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /ek.más.sɔː/ → /ekˈmas.so/ → /ekˈma.so/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /ek.más.sɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /ekˈmas.so/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /ekˈmas.so/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /ekˈmas.so/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /ekˈma.so/

Verb

[edit]

ἐκμάσσω • (ekmássō)

  1. to wipe off, wipe away
  2. (middle voice) to wipe away one's tears
  3. to mould or model in wax or plaster
