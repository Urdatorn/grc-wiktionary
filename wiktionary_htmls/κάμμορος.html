Ancient Greek

[edit]

Alternative forms

[edit]
  • κάσμορος (kásmoros)

Etymology

[edit]

Contracted from κατα- (kata-) +‎ μόρος (móros).

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /kám.mo.ros/ → /ˈkam.mo.ros/ → /ˈka.mo.ros/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /kám.mo.ros/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /ˈkam.mo.ros/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /ˈkam.mo.ros/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /ˈkam.mo.ros/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /ˈka.mo.ros/

Adjective

[edit]

κάμμορος • (kámmoros) m or f (neuter κάμμορον); second declension

  1. (Epic) ill-fated
