Ancient Greek

[edit]

Alternative forms

[edit]
  • ἐκπλώω (ekplṓō) — Ionic

Etymology

[edit]

From ἐκ- (ek-, “from”) +‎ πλέω (pléō, “sail”).

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /ek.plé.ɔː/ → /ekˈple.o/ → /ekˈple.o/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /ek.plé.ɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /ekˈple.o/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /ekˈple.o/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /ekˈple.o/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /ekˈple.o/

Verb

[edit]

ἐκπλέω • (ekpléō)

  1. to sail out, sail away, weigh anchor
  2. (figurative) to go out of one's mind, lose one's mind
  3. to outsail (ships)
