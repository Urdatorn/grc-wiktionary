Ancient Greek

[edit]

Alternative forms

[edit]
  • τοσσοῦτος (tossoûtos) — Epic
  • τεσσοῦτος (tessoûtos) — Aeolic

Etymology

[edit]

From τόσος (tósos).

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /to.sûː.tos/ → /toˈsu.tos/ → /toˈsu.tos/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /to.sûː.tos/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /toˈsu.tos/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /toˈsu.tos/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /toˈsu.tos/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /toˈsu.tos/
