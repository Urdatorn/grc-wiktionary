Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

From ἐν (en) +‎ ῐ̔́στημῐ (hístēmi).

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /e.nís.tɛː.mi/ → /eˈnis.ti.mi/ → /eˈnis.ti.mi/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /e.nís.tɛː.mi/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /eˈnis.te̝.mi/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /eˈnis.ti.mi/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /eˈnis.ti.mi/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /eˈnis.ti.mi/

Verb

[edit]

ἐνίστημι • (enístēmi)

  1. to begin
  2. to be
  3. to be upon
  4. to be present
