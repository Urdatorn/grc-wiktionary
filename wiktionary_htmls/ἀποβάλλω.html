Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

From ἀπο- (apo-) +‎ βάλλω (bállō)

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /a.po.bál.lɔː/ → /a.poˈβal.lo/ → /a.poˈva.lo/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /a.po.bál.lɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /a.poˈbal.lo/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /a.poˈβal.lo/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /a.poˈval.lo/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /a.poˈva.lo/

Verb

[edit]

ἀποβάλλω • (apobállō)

  1. to throw away, discard
  2. (Byzantine) to march out
  3. (Byzantine, medicine, middle voice) to miscarry (involuntarily abort a fetus)
