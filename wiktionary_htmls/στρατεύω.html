Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

στρᾰτός (stratós, “army”) +‎ -εύω (-eúō, verbal suffix)

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /stra.těu̯.ɔː/ → /straˈte.βo/ → /straˈte.vo/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /stra.těu̯.ɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /straˈte.wo/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /straˈte.βo/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /straˈte.vo/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /straˈte.vo/

Verb

[edit]

στρᾰτεύω • (strateúō)

  1. (active voice) advance with an army or fleet, wage war
  2. (middle voice) serve in the army
