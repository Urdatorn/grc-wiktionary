Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

Equivalent to ἀπόστασις (apóstasis) +‎ -έω (-éō).

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /a.pos.ta.té.ɔː/ → /a.pos.taˈte.o/ → /a.pos.taˈte.o/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /a.pos.ta.té.ɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /a.pos.taˈte.o/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /a.pos.taˈte.o/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /a.pos.taˈte.o/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /a.pos.taˈte.o/

Verb

[edit]

ἀποστατέω • (apostatéō)

  1. (with genitive) to stand aloof from, to be absent
