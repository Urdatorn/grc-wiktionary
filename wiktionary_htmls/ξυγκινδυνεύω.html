Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /ksyŋ.kin.dyː.něu̯.ɔː/ → /ksyɲ.ɟin.dyˈne.βo/ → /ksiɲ.ɟin.diˈne.vo/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /ksyŋ.kin.dyː.něu̯.ɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /ksyŋ.kin.dyˈne.wo/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /ksyɲ.ɟin.dyˈne.βo/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /ksyɲ.ɟin.dyˈne.vo/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /ksiɲ.ɟin.diˈne.vo/

Verb

[edit]

ξυγκινδῡνεύω • (xunkindūneúō)

  1. Old Attic form of συγκινδῡνεύω (sunkindūneúō)
