Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

ἐξ- (ex-) +‎ ἐρᾱ́ω (erā́ō, “to pour forth, vomit”)

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /ek.se.rɔ̂ː/ → /ek.seˈro/ → /ek.seˈro/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /ek.se.rɔ̂ː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /ek.seˈro/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /ek.seˈro/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /ek.seˈro/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /ek.seˈro/

Verb

[edit]

ἐξερᾱ́ω • (exerā́ō)

  1. to evacuate, esp. by purge or vomit
  2. to disgorge
  3. to pour out, let fall
