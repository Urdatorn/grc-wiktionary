Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

From ἐκ- (ek-) +‎ λεπτύ̄νω (leptú̄nō).

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /e.klep.ty̌ː.nɔː/ → /e.klepˈty.no/ → /e.klepˈti.no/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /e.klep.ty̌ː.nɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /e.klepˈty.no/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /e.klepˈty.no/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /e.klepˈty.no/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /e.klepˈti.no/

Verb

[edit]

ἐκλεπτύ̄νω • (ekleptú̄nō)

  1. to make very thin, reduce to a fine state
  2. to refine
  3. (intransitive) to become subtle and light
