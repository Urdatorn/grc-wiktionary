Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

στάσις (stásis) +‎ -άζω (-ázō)

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /sta.si.áz.dɔː/ → /sta.siˈa.zo/ → /sta.siˈa.zo/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /sta.si.áz.dɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /sta.siˈa.zo/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /sta.siˈa.zo/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /sta.siˈa.zo/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /sta.siˈa.zo/

Verb

[edit]

στᾰσῐάζω • (stasiázō)

  1. to rebel, revolt
  2. to form a party or faction
  3. to be at odds, quarrel, be at discord
  4. to revolutionize, throw into confusion
