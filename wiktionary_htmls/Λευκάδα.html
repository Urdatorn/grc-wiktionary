Ancient Greek

[edit]

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /leu̯.ká.da/ → /leɸˈka.ða/ → /lefˈka.ða/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /leu̯.ká.da/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /lewˈka.da/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /leɸˈka.ða/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /lefˈka.ða/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /lefˈka.ða/

Proper noun

[edit]

Λευκάδᾰ • (Leukáda)

  1. accusative singular of Λευκάς (Leukás)
