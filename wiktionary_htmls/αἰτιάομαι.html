Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

αἰτίᾱ (aitíā) +‎ -άομαι (-áomai)

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /ai̯.ti.á.o.mai̯/ → /ɛ.tiˈa.o.mɛ/ → /e.tiˈa.o.me/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /ai̯.ti.á.o.mai̯/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /ɛ.tiˈa.o.mɛ/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /ɛ.tiˈa.o.mɛ/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /e.tiˈa.o.me/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /e.tiˈa.o.me/

Verb

[edit]

αἰτῐάομαι • (aitiáomai)

  1. to accuse
  2. to blame
  3. to charge
