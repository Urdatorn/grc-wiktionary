Ancient Greek

[edit]

Alternative forms

[edit]
  • ἀνακάω (anakáō) — Attic

Etymology

[edit]

From ἀνα- (ana-) +‎ καίω (kaíō).

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /a.na.kǎi̯.ɔː/ → /a.naˈcɛ.o/ → /a.naˈce.o/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /a.na.kǎi̯.ɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /a.naˈkɛ.o/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /a.naˈcɛ.o/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /a.naˈce.o/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /a.naˈce.o/

Verb

[edit]

ἀνᾰκαίω • (anakaíō)

  1. to light up
  2. to fire up (with anger)
