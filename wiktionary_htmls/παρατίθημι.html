Ancient Greek

[edit]

Alternative forms

[edit]
  • παρτίθημι (partíthēmi) — poetic

Etymology

[edit]

παρα- (para-) +‎ τίθημι (títhēmi)

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /pa.ra.tí.tʰɛː.mi/ → /pa.raˈti.θi.mi/ → /pa.raˈti.θi.mi/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /pa.ra.tí.tʰɛː.mi/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /pa.raˈti.tʰe̝.mi/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /pa.raˈti.θi.mi/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /pa.raˈti.θi.mi/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /pa.raˈti.θi.mi/

Verb

[edit]

πᾰρᾰτίθημῐ • (paratíthēmi)

  1. to place or set by or before
  2. to serve (food and drink)
  3. to provide, give
  4. (in middle) to put up as a stake; to wager, risk, stake
