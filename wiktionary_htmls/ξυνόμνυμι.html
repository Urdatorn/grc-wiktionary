Ancient Greek

[edit]

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /ksy.nóm.nyː.mi/ → /ksyˈnom.ny.mi/ → /ksiˈnom.ni.mi/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /ksy.nóm.nyː.mi/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /ksyˈnom.ny.mi/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /ksyˈnom.ny.mi/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /ksyˈnom.ny.mi/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /ksiˈnom.ni.mi/

Verb

[edit]

ξῠνόμνῡμῐ • (xunómnūmi)

  1. Old Attic form of συνόμνῡμι (sunómnūmi)
