Ancient Greek

[edit]

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /da.krý.ɔːn/ → /ðaˈkry.on/ → /ðaˈkri.on/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /da.krý.ɔːn/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /daˈkry.on/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /ðaˈkry.on/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /ðaˈkry.on/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /ðaˈkri.on/

Participle

[edit]

δακρύων • (dakrúōn) m (feminine δακρῡ́ουσᾰ, neuter δακρῦον); first/third declension

  1. present active participle of δακρῡ́ω (dakrū́ō)
