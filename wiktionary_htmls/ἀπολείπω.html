Ancient Greek

[edit]

Alternative forms

[edit]
  • ἀπολιμπάνω (apolimpánō)

Etymology

[edit]

From ἀπο- (apo-) +‎ λείπω (leípō).

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /a.po.lěː.pɔː/ → /a.poˈli.po/ → /a.poˈli.po/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /a.po.lěː.pɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /a.poˈli.po/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /a.poˈli.po/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /a.poˈli.po/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /a.poˈli.po/

Verb

[edit]

ἀπολείπω • (apoleípō)

  1. to leave (behind), desert
  2. (in passive) to remain
