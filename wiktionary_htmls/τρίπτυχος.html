Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

From τρι- (tri-) +‎ πτυχή (ptukhḗ) +‎ -ος (-os)

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /tríp.ty.kʰos/ → /ˈtrip.ty.xos/ → /ˈtrip.ti.xos/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /tríp.ty.kʰos/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /ˈtrip.ty.kʰos/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /ˈtrip.ty.xos/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /ˈtrip.ty.xos/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /ˈtrip.ti.xos/

Adjective

[edit]

τρίπτῠχος • (tríptukhos) m or f (neuter τρίπτῠχον); second declension

  1. threefold
