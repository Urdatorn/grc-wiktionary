Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

A compound of φίλος (phílos, “beloved, loving”) + λόγος (lógos, “word, talk, reason”).

Adjective

[edit]

φῐλόλογος • (philólogos) m or f (neuter φῐλόλογον); second declension

  1. that loves to speak, loquacious
  2. loving logic
  3. passionate about learning, educated, learned
  4. student of words
