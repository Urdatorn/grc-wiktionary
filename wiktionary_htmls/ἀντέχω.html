Ancient Greek

[edit]

Alternative forms

[edit]
  • ἀντίσχω (antískhō)

Etymology

[edit]

ἀντι- (anti-) +‎ ἔχω (ékhō)

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /an.té.kʰɔː/ → /anˈte.xo/ → /anˈde.xo/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /an.té.kʰɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /anˈte.kʰo/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /anˈte.xo/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /anˈte.xo/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /anˈde.xo/

Verb

[edit]

ἀντέχω • (antékhō)

  1. to hold out, keep
  2. to withstand, endure, persevere
  3. to prevail, be prevalent
  4. (middle voice) to cling to, adhere to, keep close to
  5. (middle voice) to support, care for
  6. (middle voice) to resist
