Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

From ἐπανα- (epana-, “re”) +‎ λύω (lúō, “loosen”).

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /e.pa.na.ly̌ː.ɔː/ → /e.pa.naˈly.o/ → /e.pa.naˈli.o/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /e.pa.na.ly̌ː.ɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /e.pa.naˈly.o/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /e.pa.naˈly.o/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /e.pa.naˈly.o/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /e.pa.naˈli.o/

Verb

[edit]

ἐπᾰνᾰλῡ́ω • (epanalū́ō)

  1. to return
