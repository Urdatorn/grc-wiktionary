Ancient Greek

[edit]

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /eu̯k.sá.me.nos/ → /eɸkˈsa.me.nos/ → /efkˈsa.me.nos/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /eu̯k.sá.me.nos/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /ewkˈsa.me.nos/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /eɸkˈsa.me.nos/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /efkˈsa.me.nos/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /efkˈsa.me.nos/

Participle

[edit]

εὐξάμενος • (euxámenos) m (feminine εὐξᾰμένη, neuter εὐξάμενον); first/second declension

  1. aorist middle participle of εὔχομαι (eúkhomai)
