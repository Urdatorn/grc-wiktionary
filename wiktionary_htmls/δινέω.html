Ancient Greek

[edit]

Alternative forms

[edit]
  • δῑνεύω (dīneúō), δῑ́νημι (dī́nēmi), δῑ́ννημι (dī́nnēmi)

Etymology

[edit]

From δῑ́νη (dī́nē)

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /diː.né.ɔː/ → /ðiˈne.o/ → /ðiˈne.o/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /diː.né.ɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /diˈne.o/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /ðiˈne.o/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /ðiˈne.o/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /ðiˈne.o/

Verb

[edit]

δῑνέω • (dīnéō)

  1. to whirl
