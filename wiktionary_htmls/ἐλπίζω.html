Ancient Greek

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

From ἐλπίς (elpís, “hope”) +‎ -ίζω (-ízō). Alternatively, the root ἐλπίδ- "hope" from ἐλπίς has merged with the *y progressive aspect marker in the present tense system.

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /el.píz.dɔː/ → /elˈpi.zo/ → /elˈpi.zo/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /el.píz.dɔː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /elˈpi.zo/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /elˈpi.zo/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /elˈpi.zo/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /elˈpi.zo/

Verb

[edit]

ἐλπίζω • (elpízō)

  1. to hope for, to look for, expect
    • 467 BCE, Aeschylus, Seven Against Thebes 589
    • 458 BCE, Aeschylus, The Libation Bearers 539
    • 430 BCE – 354 BCE, Xenophon, Memorabilia 4.3.17
    • 343 BCE, Demosthenes, On the False Embassy 102
    • 460 BCE – 420 BCE, Herodotus, Histories 3.143
    • 480 BCE – 411 BCE, Antiphon of Rhamnus, First Tetralogy 3.6
    • 460 BCE – 395 BCE, Thucydides, History of the Peloponnesian War 4.71
    • 445 BCE – 380 BCE, Lysias, For Mantitheus 2
    • 445 BCE – 380 BCE, Lysias, Funeral Oration 21
    • 409 BCE, Sophocles, Philoctetes 629
    • 430 BCE – 354 BCE, Xenophon, Agesilaus 7.6
    • 460 BCE – 420 BCE, Herodotus, Histories 3.151
    • 460 BCE – 395 BCE, Thucydides, History of the Peloponnesian War 2.53
    • 497 BCE – 405 BCE, Sophocles, Electra 1281
    • 430 BCE, Euripides, Herakles' Children 1051
    • 497 BCE – 405 BCE, Sophocles, Electra 963
    • 406 BCE, Sophocles, Oedipus at Colonus 1105
    1. (of evils) to look for, fear
      • 497 BCE – 405 BCE, Sophocles, Women of Trachis 111
      • 497 BCE – 405 BCE, Sophocles, Ajax 799
      • 445 BCE – 380 BCE, Lysias, Against Eratosthenes 70
      • 414 BCE, Aristophanes, The Birds 956
      • 460 BCE – 420 BCE, Herodotus, Histories 8.12
      • 413 BCE, Euripides, Ion 348
      • 460 BCE – 420 BCE, Herodotus, Histories 1.77
      • 460 BCE – 420 BCE, Herodotus, Histories 8.53
    2. (with present infinitive) to think, deem, suppose, believe
      • 460 BCE – 420 BCE, Herodotus, Histories 1.30
      • 460 BCE – 420 BCE, Herodotus, Histories 1.22
      • 460 BCE – 420 BCE, Herodotus, Histories 1.27
      • 467 BCE, Aeschylus, Seven Against Thebes 76
      • 458 BCE, Aeschylus, The Libation Bearers 187
      • 425 BCE, Euripides, Andromache 720
      • 380 BCE, Plato, The Republic 573C
      • 445 BCE – 380 BCE, Lysias, Against Philon 27
      • 384 BCE – 322 BCE, Demosthenes, First Philippic 7
    3. (with dative) to hope in
      • 460 BCE – 395 BCE, Thucydides, History of the Peloponnesian War 3.97
      • 70 CE – 110 CE, The Gospel of Matthew 12:21
      • 300 BCE – 200 BCE, Septuagint, 2 Kings 18.5
      • 50 CE – 100 CE, The Gospel of John 5:45
      • New Testament, Epistle to the Romans 15:12
      • New Testament, First Epistle of Peter 3:5
