Ancient Greek

[edit]

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /ɡe.ɡó.nɛː.te/ → /ʝeˈɣo.ni.te/ → /ʝeˈɣo.ni.te/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /ɡe.ɡó.nɛː.te/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /ɡeˈɡo.ne̝.te/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /ʝeˈɣo.ni.te/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /ʝeˈɣo.ni.te/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /ʝeˈɣo.ni.te/

Verb

[edit]

γεγόνητε • (gegónēte)

  1. second-person plural perfect active subjunctive of γίγνομαι (gígnomai)
