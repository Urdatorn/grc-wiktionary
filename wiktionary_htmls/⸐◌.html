Ancient Greek

[edit]

Diacritical mark

[edit]

⸐◌

  1. A forked paragraphos, a termination mark that extends into the left margin and marks the end of a section of tex. The forked paragraphos demarcates a different level of text break than the regular paragraphos ⟨⸏◌⟩.

Usage notes

[edit]

The reversed ⟨⸑◌⟩ is a rare variant.
