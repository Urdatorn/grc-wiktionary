Ancient Greek

[edit]

Pronunciation

[edit]
 
  • IPA(key): /ɛ̂ː/ → /i/ → /i/
  • (5th BCE Attic) IPA(key): /ɛ̂ː/
  • (1st CE Egyptian) IPA(key): /e̝/
  • (4th CE Koine) IPA(key): /i/
  • (10th CE Byzantine) IPA(key): /i/
  • (15th CE Constantinopolitan) IPA(key): /i/

Verb

[edit]

ἦ • (ê)

  1. first-person singular imperfect of εἰμί (eimí): I was
  2. third-person singular imperfect/aorist of ἠμί (ēmí): (s)he said

Conjunction

[edit]

ἦ • (ê)

  1. or

Usage notes

[edit]

The pair ἤ... ἦ (ḗ... ê) means ‘either... or...’ or ‘whether... or...’.

Alternative forms

[edit]
  • ἦε (êe) — Epic
